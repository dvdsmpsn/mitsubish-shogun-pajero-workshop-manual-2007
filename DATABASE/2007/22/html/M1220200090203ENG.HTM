REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< SHIFT RAIL AND SHIT FORK INSTALLATION



Apply the specified grease to the outer periphery of the shift fork mounting portion of the shift rail and then assemble the shift fork and shift rail.
Specified lubricant:
Mitsubishi Part No. 0101011 or equivalent

>>B<< SPRING PIN INSTALLATION



Install the spring pin with its split toward the forward end of the transfer.
