REAR HEATER UNIT <SHORT WHEELBASE OPTION>




The rear heater unit has been adopted to the floor console to improve the comfort of the rear seat.


REAR HEATER UNIT <VEHICLES WITH DUAL A/C>




The two-level flow full air mix heater has been installed to reduce noise.


Air flows inside the rear heater unit along two levels: one that goes through the heater core, and one that bypasses the heater core, and temperature is controlled by a single air mix damper. In addition, the two-level flow full air mix heater has a low air flow resistance, so that the air flow volume could be increased and operating noise could be reduced.
