Interior design with safe material has been adopted, achieving more safe and comfortable interior.

  • Push-open type sunglasses holder has been installed. <Vehicles without sunroof>
  • Energy absorption pad and tube have been adopted to the inside of headlining. This reduces head injuries upon the secondary impact against the cabin during a collision, and improves safety. <Vehicles without SRS curtain airbag>
  • Headlining which absorbs and dissolves chemical substances has been adopted.
  • Chemical substances such as formaldehyde and organic solvent generated from the interior parts have been minimised by the refined material and method, improving amenity.
