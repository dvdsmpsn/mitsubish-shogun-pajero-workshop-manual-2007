By using the auto-cruise control, the driver can drive at preferred speeds in a range of approximately 40 to 165 km/h without depressing the accelerator pedal.
For this auto-cruise control system, in conjunction with the electronically-controlled throttle valve system, the engine-A/T-ECU electronically controls the throttle valve <6G7>.
For this auto-cruise control system, in conjunction with the electronically-controlled fuel injection system, the engine-ECU electronically controls the fuel injection system <4M4>.
