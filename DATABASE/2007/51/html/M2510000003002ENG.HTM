CONSTRUCTION DIAGRAM





  • Aerodynamic structure from the front bumper, to the front overfender, to the side air dam (side sill garnish), to the rear overfender and to the rear bumper keeps the off-road vehicle image as a RV, at the same time the optimized shape of the related parts improves aerodynamic performance.
  • The front/rear overfenders offering the hopes of all round driving have been installed to appeal a sporty and robust wheeling image with a sense of continuity with front bumper, side air dams (side sill garnishes) and rear bumper.
  • The front/rear overfenders have been manufactured as a unit which covers front bumper/rear bumper respectively for thinned better appearance.
  • Coloured material (material colour: grey) structure has been adopted for side air dams (side sill garnishes). The grained surface makes stone chipping or scratches by passenger's access inconspicuous.
