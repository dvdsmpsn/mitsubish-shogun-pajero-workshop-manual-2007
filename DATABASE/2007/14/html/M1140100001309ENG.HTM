RADIATOR CAP VALVE OPENING PRESSURE CHECK


M11401000013ENG0000010000



1.
noteBe sure that the cap is clean before testing. Rust or other foreign material on the cap seal will cause an improper reading.
Use a cap adapter to attach the cap to the tester.
2. Increase the pressure until the indicator of the gauge stops moving.

    Minimum limit: 83 kPa
    Standard value: 93 - 123 kPa
3. Replace the radiator cap if the reading does not remain at or above the limit.
