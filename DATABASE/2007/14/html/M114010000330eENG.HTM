REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> RADIATOR UPPER HOSE DISCONNECTION



Make mating marks on the radiator lower hose and the hose clamp. Disconnect the radiator lower hose.

<<B>> ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR REMOVAL



Use special tool water temperature sensor wrench (MB992042) to remove the engine coolant water temperature sensor.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< O-RING INSTALLATION


cautionCare must be taken not to permit engine oil or other greases to adhere to the O-ring.
Insert the O-ring to pipe, and coat the outer circumference of the O-ring with water.

>>B<< ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR INSTALLATION




Apply the sealant to the thread of engine coolant temperature sensor. In the same manner as for removal, using special tool water temp sensor wrench (MB992042), tighten to the specified torque.

  • Specified sealant: 3M Nut Locking Part No. 4171 or equivalent
  • Tightening torque: 30 ± 9 N·m

>>C<< ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE GAUGE UNIT INSTALLATION



Apply the sealant to the thread of engine coolant temperature gauge unit, and then tighten to the specified torque.

    Specified sealant: 3M ATD Part No. 8660 or equivalent
    Tightening torque: 10.5 ± 0.5 N·m

>>D<< RADIATOR UPPER HOSE CONNECTION



1. Insert the radiator lower hose as far as the projection of the cooling water inlet hose fitting.
2. Align the mating marks on the radiator lower hose and hose clamp, and then connect the radiator lower hose.
