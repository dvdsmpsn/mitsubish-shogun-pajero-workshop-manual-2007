1. Radio
Tune the radio to a local broadcasting station and check the following:
(1)Operate the volume, tone, balance and fader controls, etc.
(2)Pull out the pushbuttons, dial another station and set each pushbuttons.
(3)Operate the AM/FM switch.
2. Tape player
Insert a cassette tape in the tape player and check as follows:
(1)Check the operation of the tape feeder and rewind.
(2)Check the ejection.
(3)Check the operation of volume, tone, balance and fader controls, etc.
