INSPECTION AND SERVICE AFTER FORDING A STREAM


M10001000155ENG0000010000


After fording a stream, check the following points. If abnormal conditions is evident, clean, replace or lubricate.

  • Check of water, mud, sand, etc. in the rear brake drum, clutch housing, starter motor, brake pipe and fuel pipe.
  • Check for water in the fluid or oil inside the front differential, rear differential, transmission and transfer.
  • Check all boots and breather hoses for cracks and damage.
