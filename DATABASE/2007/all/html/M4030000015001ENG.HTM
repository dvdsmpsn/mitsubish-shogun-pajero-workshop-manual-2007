REAR END CROSSMEMBER

NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



cautionBecause the rear floor pan rear (short wheelbase) or the rear floor pan third seat rear (long wheelbase) interferes with the rear end outer crossmember, turn it up.
1. Replace the rear end crossmember outer as a whole, and partially replace the rear end crossmember inner.
Cut both the rear end crossmember outer and the rear end crossmember inner 70 ± 10 mm inward from the tip of the quarter outer extension lower.

2. Remove the rear end crossmember outer remaining on the body side.

INSTALLATION



1. Cut and install the new rear end crossmember inner by aligning it with the body side.

2. For the new rear end crossmember outer, because the quarter outer extension lower is inserted into between the rear end crossmember outer and the weatherstrip channel, cut the 2 welding points of the weatherstrip channel to provide a clearance.

3. When installing the rear end crossmember outer, insert the left side of the rear end crossmember outer more than the installation point. After that, insert the right side, and return the part to the installation point.
