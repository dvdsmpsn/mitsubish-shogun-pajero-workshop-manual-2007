Inspection Procedure 2: When the Clutch Pedal is Depressed, Auto-cruise Control is not Cancelled <4M4>.


M11702000047ENG0000010001




OPERATION


This circuit indicates the operation status of the clutch pedal position switch. When the clutch switch is ON (clutch pedal is depressed), the voltage of engine-ECU terminal number 95 will indicate 0 volt.

DIAGNOSIS CODE SET CONDITIONS


The cause is probably a malfunction of the clutch switch circuit.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Damaged harness or connector.
  • Malfunction of the clutch switch.
  • Malfunction of the engine-ECU.

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III data list.


Item 07: Clutch switch [Refer to data list reference table <4M4> ].

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Go to Step 2.

STEP 2. Check the clutch switch.



(1)Disconnect clutch switch connector D-33.

(2)Measure the continuity between the terminals.
Measurement condition
Terminal connector of tester
Specified condition
When clutch pedal is depressed.
1 - 2
Continuity (less than 2 Ω)
When clutch pedal is not depressed.
1 - 2
Open circuit

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Replace the clutch switch (Refer to GROUP 21A - Clutch Pedal ). Then go to Step 9.

STEP 3. Connectors check: D-33 clutch switch connector, D-215 engine-ECU connector, D-231 and D-241 intermediate connectors <L.H. drive vehicles>.





Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair or replace the faulty connector. Then go to Step 9.

STEP 4. Check the harness between clutch switch connector D-33 terminal No.1 and engine-ECU connector D-215 terminal No.95.




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6 <L.H. drive vehicles>.
Go to Step 5 <R.H. drive vehicles>.
Repair the harness wire. Then go to Step 9.

STEP 5. Connector check: D-14 earth connector <R.H. drive vehicles>.



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Repair or replace the faulty connector. Then go to Step 9.

STEP 6. Check the harness between clutch switch connector D-33 terminal No.2 and earth.




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 7.
Repair the harness wire. Then go to Step 9.

STEP 7. M.U.T.-III data list.


Item 07: Clutch switch [Refer to data list reference table <4M4> ].

Q. Is the check result normal?

It can be assumed that this malfunction is intermittent (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
Replace the engine-ECU (Refer to GROUP 13Ba - Engine-ECU ). Then go to Step 9 .

STEP 8. Retest the system.


Q. When the Clutch Pedal is Depressed, Auto-cruise Control is Cancelled?

It can be assumed that this malfunction is intermittent (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
Replace the engine-ECU (Refer to GROUP 13Ba - Engine-ECU ). Then go to Step 9 .

STEP 9. Retest the system.


Q. When the Clutch Pedal is Depressed, Auto-cruise Control is Cancelled?

The procedure is complete.
Return to Step 1.
