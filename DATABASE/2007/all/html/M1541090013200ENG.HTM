Inspection Procedure D-5: The Door Lamps does not Illuminate Normally.


M15410900132ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The door lamps are energised through the ETACS-ECU. If a door is opened, the relevant door lamp will illuminate.
If this function does not work normally, these input signal circuit(s) or the ETACS-ECU may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the door lamp bulbs
  • Malfunction of the door switches
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


Step 1. Connector check: F-07 door lamp (front: LH) connector, F-13 door lamp (front: RH) connector, F-19 door lamp (rear: LH) connector, F-27 door lamp (rear: RH) connector






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 2. Check the bulbs of the door lamp.


Check that the door lamp bulbs are not burned out.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Replace the door lamp bulb.

STEP 3. Connector check: E-17 door switch (front: LH) connector, E-29 door switch (front: RH) connector, E-41 door switch (rear: LH) connector, E-32 door switch (rear: RH) connector






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 4. Check the door switch.


Check the door switch, which corresponds to the defective door lamp (Refer to GROUP 42A - Door ).

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Replace the door lamp bulb.

STEP 5. Connector check: D-419 ETACS-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 6. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.6 to E-17 door switch (front: LH) or E-29 door switch (front: RH) connector, E-41 door switch (rear: LH) connector, E-32 door switch (rear: RH) connector terminal No.2.













note<L.H. drive vehicles> Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-133 <door switch (front: LH)>, D-132 <door switch (front: LH)>, D-234 <door switch (front: LH), door switch (rear: LH)>, D-114 <door switch (front: RH)>, D-115 <door switch (front: RH)>, D-219 <door switch (front: RH), door switch (rear: RH)>, E-42 <door switch (rear: LH)>, E-43 <door switch (rear: RH)>, joint connector D-34, junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.



note<R.H. drive vehicles> Prior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-133 <door switch (front: LH)>, D-132 <door switch (front: LH)>, D-234 <door switch (front: LH), door switch (rear: LH)>, D-114 <door switch (front: RH)>, D-115 <door switch (front: RH)>, D-221 <door switch (front: RH)>, E-42 <door switch (rear: LH)>, E-43 <door switch (rear: RH)>, joint connector D-34 <except for door switch (rear: RH)>, junction block connector D-410 <except for door switch (rear: RH)>, junction block connector D-422 <door switch (rear: RH)>, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the wiring harness.
