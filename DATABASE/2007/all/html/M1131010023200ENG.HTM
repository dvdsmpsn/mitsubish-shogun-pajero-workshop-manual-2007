Code No. P0443: Purge Control Solenoid Valve System


M11310100232ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • Power is supplied to the purge control solenoid valve (terminal No. 2) from the engine control relay (terminal No. 1).
  • The engine-A/T-ECU (terminal No. 23) makes the power transistor in the unit be in ON position, and that makes currents go on the purge control solenoid valve (terminal No. 1).

FUNCTION



  • In response to a signal from the engine-A/T-ECU, the purge control solenoid valve controls the flow rate of the purge air to be introduced into the surge tank.

TROUBLE JUDGMENT


Check Conditions
  • Ignition switch is in ON position.
  • Battery positive voltage is 10 V or higher.
Judgment Criterion
  • The surge voltage (system voltage +2 V) of solenoid coil is not detected when the purge control solenoid valve is turned in OFF position from ON.

PROBABLE CAUSE



  • Failed purge control solenoid valve
  • Open/short circuit in purge control solenoid value circuit or loose connector contact
  • Failed engine-A/T-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III actuator test
