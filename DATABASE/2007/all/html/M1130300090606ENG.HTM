COMMON RAIL CHECK


M11303000906ENG0000010000


cautionHigh pressure fuel is supplied to the common rail. It is dangerous to remove the rail pressure sensor and rail pressure limiter from the common rail because it may cause fuel leaks. Do not remove the rail pressure sensor and rail pressure limiter from the common rail.

1. Visually check the common rail for cracks, damage, and corrosion.
2. If an abnormality is found, replace the common rail.
