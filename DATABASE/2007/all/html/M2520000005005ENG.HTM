SECOND SEAT

The second seats offer the following advantages.

  • Separate seats (6:4 arrangement) have been adopted.
  • The seat side back folding mechanism marks it easier to fold down the seats. <Long wheelbase>
  • The Head restraint can be adjusted up and down. The L-shaped centre headrest is adopted for better rear visibility.
  • The seat back forward folding mechanism and tumble mechanism allow the luggage compartment space to be increased and also improve access to and from the third seat.
  • The arm rests are provided with cup holders to improve merchantability.
  • The child seat fixing bar complied with top tether anchorage and lower anchorage has been installed for all models as a standard equipment.
