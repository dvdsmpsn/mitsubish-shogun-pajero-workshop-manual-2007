G AND YAW RATE SENSOR INITIALISATION


M13506200060ENG0000010000


When the G and yaw rate sensor is replaced, the learned neutral point of the G and yaw rate sensor is erased, and ASC OFF indicator illuminates to warn that the ASTC-ECU is not initialised yet, and diagnosis code No.81 and No.82 are output. In this case, use the following procedure to initialise the G and yaw rate sensor.
1. Place the vehicle on a level surface.
2. After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK"(OFF) position, place M.U.T.-III
3. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position.
4. Perform Item No.16 of the actuator test.
5. Ensure that the ASC OFF indicator has been turned OFF.
6. Turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK"(OFF) position.
7. Remove M.U.T.-III
