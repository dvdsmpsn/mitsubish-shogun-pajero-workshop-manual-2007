Code No. P1571 Stop Lamp Switch System <4M4>


M11702000907ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • Battery positive voltage is supplied to the stop lamp switch (terminal 2 and 4).
  • When the brake pedal is depressed, battery positive voltage is applied to the engine-ECU terminal 34 and 94.

DIAGNOSIS CODE SET CONDITIONS


Check Condition
  • The auto-cruise control indicator lamp illuminates.
Judgement Criteria
  • Short in stop lamp switch circuit.
  • Open circuit in the brake switch circuit (between engine-ECU terminal 94 and earth).

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the stop lamp switch.
  • Damaged harness or connector.
  • Malfunction of the engine-ECU.

STEP 9. Check the stop lamp switch.


(1)Remove the stop lamp switch (Refer to GROUP 35A - Brake Pedal ).


(2)Connect an ohmmeter to the stop lamp switch between terminals 1 and 2.
(3)Check for continuity between the terminals when the plunger of the stop lamp switch is pushed in and when it is released.
OK: The stop lamp switch is operating properly if the circuit is open between terminals 1 and 2 when the plunger is pushed in to a depth of within 4.0 mm from the outer case edge surface, and if the resistance value is less than 2 Ω between terminals 1 and 2 when it is released.

OK: The stop lamp switch is operating properly if the circuit is open between terminals 3 and 4 when the plunger is released, and if resistance value is less than 2 Ω between terminals 3 and 4 when the plunger is pushed in to a depth of within 4.5 mm from the outer case edge surface.
