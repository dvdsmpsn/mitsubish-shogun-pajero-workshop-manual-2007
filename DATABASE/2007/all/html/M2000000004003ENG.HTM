With the further strengthened “tradition of PAJERO” including powerful styling, off-road driving performance, and tight turn capability, the new PAJERO has successfully satisfied the expectation and needs of the users from the 1st generation to the current PAJERO.

  • Take over the markets all over the world developed by the successive PAJEROs without betraying the brand image established so far.
  • Launch the new PAJERO, emblematic of Mitsubishi Motors into the “upper-middle-class” SUV market which accounts for the largest share in the SUV segment.
  • Aim at the regeneration of PAJERO brand by appealing the styling which is suitable for the SUV-DNA embodiment “genuine” and “king of off-road,” as well as further improved durability and power performance.
  • Represent the existence value in respect of reliability, rough-road climbing ability, towing capacity and styling by winning the evident superiority over the crossover SUV.
