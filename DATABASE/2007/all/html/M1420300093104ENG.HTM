INSPECTION PROCEDURE 7: The Window Glass Lowers Automatically while it is Rising.


M14203000931ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the sliding resistance is too great when the window is being raised or the window glass encounters an object, the window glass will lower by approximately 150 mm.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Improper adjusted door window glass
  • Incorrectly installed or warped glass slider
  • Malfunction of the power window motor
  • Malfunction of the window regulator
