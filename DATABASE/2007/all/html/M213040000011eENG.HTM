The fuel system consists of the following components:

  • Fuel injector
  • Fuel delivery pipe
  • Fuel vapour canister
  • Fuel tank assembly
  • Fuel tank pump assembly
  • Fuel tank gauge unit
The fuel tank assembly has the following features.

  • The use of the fuel return less system, in which the fuel pump pressure regulator is incorporated in the fuel tank pump assembly, allows the fuel return pipe and hose to be discontinued, and reduces weight.
  • A sheet-metal fuel delivery pipe has been adopted so that its cross-sectional area change reduces fuel pressure pulsation, thereby reducing variations in the amount of fuel injection to improve exhaust gas performance.
