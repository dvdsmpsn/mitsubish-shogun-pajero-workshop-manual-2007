Inspection Procedure 10: The PTC heater (Rear floor console side) does not work <Short wheelbase option>


M15504000178ENG0000010002




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


When the PTC heater does not work under the following conditions, the PTC heater circuit system may be defective.
PTC heater operating conditions
  • Rear fan switch: ON
  • Rear A/C switch Temperature control dial: MAX HOT

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors
  • Malfunction of the PTC heater relay
  • Malfunction of the PTC heater
  • Malfunction of the rear A/C switch
  • Malfunction of the automatic A/C control panel (A/C-ECU)

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: E-26-5 PTC heater connector



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Resistance measurement at the E-26-5 PTC heater connector.



(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.

(2)Continuity between terminal 1 and body earth
OK: Continuity (Less than 2 Ω)

Go to Step 3.
