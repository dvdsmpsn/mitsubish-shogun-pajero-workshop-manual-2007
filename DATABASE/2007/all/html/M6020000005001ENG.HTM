D4. CHECK BRAKE PADS AND DISCS FOR WEAR


M60200000050ENG0000010000



1. Check the brake pad thickness through the caliper body check port.
Standard value:

    <Short wheelbase> 10.0 mm
    <Long wheelbase> 11.5 mm
Limit: 2.0 mm
2. When the thickness is less than the limit, always replace the pads at an axle set.

3. Using a micrometer, measure disc thickness at eight positions, approximately 45° apart and 10 mm in from the outer edge of the disc.
Standard value:

4. If the disc thickness is less than the limits, replace it with a new one.

BRAKE DISC RUN-OUT CHECK


1. Remove the brake assembly, and then hold it with wire.

2. Place a dial gauge approximately 5 mm from the outer circumference of the brake disc, and measure the run-out of the disc.
Limit: 0.06 mm or less
