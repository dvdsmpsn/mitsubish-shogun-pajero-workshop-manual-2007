Inspection Procedure C-4: The Fog Lamp Indicator does not Illuminate.


M15410900116ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the front fog lamp indicator or rear fog lamp indicator does not illuminate normally, connector(s), wiring harness in the CAN bus lines, the ETACS-ECU or the combination meter may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • The CAN bus line is defective.
  • The combination meter may be defective
  • The ETACS-ECU may be defective
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the front fog lamps or rear fog lamp.


Check that the front fog lamps or rear fog lamp illuminate normally.

Q. Is the headlamps in good condition?

Go to Step 2.
Refer to Inspection Procedure C-1 "The front fog lamps do not illuminate normally ." or Inspection Procedure C-2 "The rear fog lamp do not illuminate normally ."

STEP 2. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics


Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the CAN bus line (Refer to GROUP 54D - Troubleshooting ).

STEP 3. M.U.T.-III actuator test


Perform the actuator test for the combination meter, and check that the turn-signal indicator illuminates (Refer to GROUP 54Ab - Troubleshooting ).

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the ETACS-ECU.
Replace the combination meter.
