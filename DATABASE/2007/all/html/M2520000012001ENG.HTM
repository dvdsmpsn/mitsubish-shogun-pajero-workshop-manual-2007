INTERIOR TRIM

  • The interior trim is a full trim to improve quality.
  • Wide, flat scuff plates have been adopted to improve the ease of getting in and out of the vehicle.
  • The left-side quarter trim has a convenient side box and a cup holder for string small items, provided in order to increase merchant-ability. <Long wheelbase>
  • The collision energy absorbing structure that is integrated with a trim has been adopted to the pillar trim. With the structure that reduces head injuries upon the secondary impact against the cabin during a collision, the safety has been improved.
  • Material codes are shown on trim to facilitate recycling.
