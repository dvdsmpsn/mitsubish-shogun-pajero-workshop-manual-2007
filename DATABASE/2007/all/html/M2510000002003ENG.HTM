CONSTRUCTION DIAGRAM




FRONT BUMPER
  • Divided structure has been adopted for the front bumper to improve the partial replacement, the cost reduction in damaged parts replacement, and the recycle efficiency in scrapping.
  • Resin-made front bumper support brackets have been adopted to improve the alignment with the body.
  • Coloured material (material colour: grey) structure has been adopted for the front bumper bottom and the both sides of front wheelhouse splash shield and front bumper cover. The grained surface makes scratches such as stone chipping inconspicuous.
  • The front wheelhouse splash shield has been installed on the both sides of front bumper to prevent low visibility caused by the water splash during driving the bank or puddle.
REAR BUMPER
  • Divided structure has been adopted for the rear bumper to improve the partial replacement, the cost reduction in damaged parts replacement, and the recycle efficiency in scrapping.
  • Coloured material (material colour: grey) structure has been adopted for both sides of rear corner bumper and the rear bumper step on the centre upper. The grained surface makes stone chipping or scratches by passenger's access inconspicuous.
  • Resin-made rear bumper face support brackets have been adopted to improve the alignment with the body.<Long wheelbase>
