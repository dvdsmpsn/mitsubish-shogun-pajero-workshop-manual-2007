Inspection Procedure D-7: The Foot Lamps and Ceiling Lamp do not Illuminate or Extinguish Normally. <Vehicles without Keyless Entry System>


M15410900174ENG0000010002


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The ETACS-ECU operates the foot lamps and ceiling lamp in accordance with the input signals below.

  • Ignition switch (IG1)
  • Door switches
  • Back door switch
  • Driver's door lock actuator switch (unlock switch)
  • Interior lamp switch
If this function does not work normally, these input signal circuit(s) or the ETACS-ECU may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the door switches
  • Malfunction of the room lamp switches
  • Malfunction of the driver's door lock actuator switch (unlock switch)
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

STEP 5. Check the illumination of the foot lamps and the ceiling lamp.



  • Check that the foot lamps and ceiling lamp light up/off when the interior lamp switch is turned to the ON/OFF position.

Q. Which of the foot lamps or the ceiling lamp does no illuminate or extinguish?

YES (All of the lamps illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (The front foot lamp (RH or LH) does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 6.
NO (The rear foot lamp does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 9.
NO (The ceiling lamp does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 13.
