AIR DUCT AND AIR CLEANER


M21500000040ENG0000010000



  • The air duct and air cleaner basically have the same system as those of the conventional PAJERO. The intake port of the air duct is located at the top of the radiator to provide a layout which effectively draws in the cooling air. This boosts engine output performance and reduces the amount of intake air noise inside the passenger compartment.
  • For the air cleaner intake duct, the resonator has been added to reduce intake noise <6G7>.
  • A pre-cleaner has been provided to reduce the tendency of the air cleaner element to become blocked quickly <4M4>.
