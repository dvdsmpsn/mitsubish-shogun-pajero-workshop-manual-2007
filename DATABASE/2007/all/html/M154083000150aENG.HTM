Diagnosis Item 10: Diagnose the lines between the joint connectors (CAN1 and CAN2).


M15408300015ENG0000010000


cautionWhen servicing a CAN bus line, earth yourself by touching a metal object such as an unpainted water pipe. If you fail to do, a component connected to the CAN bus line may be broken.


FUNCTION


The diagnostic result demonstrates that "Diagnose the lines between the joint connectors (CAN1 and CAN2)" when the M.U.T.-III checks the periodically sent data from each ECU and cannot receive the ASTC-ECU and the combination meter data.

TROUBLE JUDGEMENT CONDITIONS


M.U.T.-III judges the trouble when the periodically sent data from ASTC-ECU and the combination meter cannot be received and sent.

PROBABLE CAUSE


Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-230 intermediate connector, D-03 joint connector (CAN1) and D-22 joint connector (CAN2)






cautionThe strand end of the twist wire should be within 10 cm from the connector. For details refer to .
When checking the joint connector, ensure that its wiring harness side and its short pins are not damaged.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector. Replace the joint connector as necessary.

STEP 2. Resistance measurement at D-03 joint connector (CAN1) and D-230 intermediate connector.






cautionA digital multimeter should be used. For details refer to .
cautionThe test wiring harness should be used. For details refer to .
(1)Disconnect the joint connector (CAN1) and the intermediate connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the LOCK (OFF) position.
(3)
cautionWhen measuring the resistance, disconnect the negative battery terminal. For details refer to .
Ensure that the negative battery terminal is disconnected.

(4)Continuity between D-03 joint connector (CAN1) terminal No.11 and D-230 intermediate connector terminal No.13
OK: Continuity (2 Ω or less)

(5)Continuity between D-03 joint connector (CAN1) terminal No.22 and D-230 intermediate connector terminal No.12
OK: Continuity (2 Ω or less)
cautionStrictly observe the specified wiring harness repair procedure. For details refer to .

Q. Is the check result normal?

<All the resistances measure 2 Ω or less> Go to Step 3.
<Either or all of the resistances measure more than 2 Ω> Repair the wiring harness between joint connector (CAN1) and intermediate connector (D-230).

(3)
