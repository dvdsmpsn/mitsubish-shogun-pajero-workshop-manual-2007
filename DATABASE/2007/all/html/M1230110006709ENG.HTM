Inspection Procedure 16: Shift switch assembly system


M12301100067ENG0000010000




OPERATION


The shift switch assembly detects the shift range (sport mode) which the driver has selected, and sends the information to the engine-A/T-ECU.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The cause is probably a malfunction of the shift switch and engine-A/T-ECU.
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of shift switch assembly
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Malfunction of the engine-A/T-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III diagnosis code


Either of diagnosis codes P1770 (P0705) or P1771 (P0705) is set, inhibitor switch is defective.
