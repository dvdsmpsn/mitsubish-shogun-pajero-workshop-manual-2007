THROTTLE VALVE CONTROL SERVO CHECK


M11303000907ENG0000010000


OPERATION CHECK


1. Remove the air intake hose from the throttle body.
2. Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position.
3. Make sure that the throttle valve is open.
4. Turn the ignition switch to "OFF" position.

CHECK THE COIL RESISTANCE



1. Disconnect the electronic - controlled throttle valve connector.
2. Measure resistance between terminal No. 5 and No. 6 at the throttle valve control servo connector.
Standard value: 0.3 - 100 Ω (at 20 °C)
3. If resistance is outside the standard value, replace the throttle body assembly.
