BACK DOOR ASSY



  • A back door with an easy-to-operate side opening mechanism has been adopted in all models.
  • The gas spring type back door stopper has been adopted.
  • The license plate carrier has been installed.

BACK DOOR STOPPER





  • The gas spring type back door stopper has been adopted, which enables to keep the back door stationary at a halfway position during the back door opening/closing on the flat.
  • The lock mechanism which enables to hold a full-open state on the slope by operating the lever right and left has been adopted.

LICENSE PLATE CARRIER




The pin to hold the full-open state of the license plate carrier and the holder to keep the closed state have been installed for convenience during a spare tyre replacement.
