Inspection Procedure 5: Check the power supply and earth circuit to the immobilizer-ECU. <3,200>


M15410700020ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The power supply and earth circuit to the immobilizer -ECU may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES


Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-126 immobilizer-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 2. Voltage measurement at immobilizer-ECU connector D-126 in order to check the battery circuit of power supply system to the immobilizer-ECU.




(1)Disconnect the immobilizer-ECU connector D-126 and measure the voltage at immobilizer-ECU connector D-126 harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to “ON” position.

(3)Measure the voltage between terminal 1 and earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Connector check: B-15X engine control relay connector


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 4. Check the wiring harness between B-15X engine control relay connector terminal No. 1 and D-126 immobilizer-ECU connector terminal No.1.









notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check joint connector D-113, intermediate connector D-229, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply line for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 5. Resistance measurement at immobilizer-ECU connector D-126 to check the earth circuit to the immobilizer-ECU connector.




(1)Disconnect the immobilizer-ECU connector D-126 and measure the resistance at immobilizer-ECU connector D-126 harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to “ON” position.

(3)Measure the resistance between terminal 2 and earth.
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less)

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Go to Step 6.

STEP 6. Check the wiring harness between D-126 immobilizer-ECU connector terminal No.2 and earth.





  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Repair the wiring harness.
