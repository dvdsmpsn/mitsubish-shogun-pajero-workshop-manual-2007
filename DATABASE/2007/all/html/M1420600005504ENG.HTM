INSPECTION PROCEDURE 4: Sunroof Timer Function does not work Normally.


M14206000055ENG0000010002


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the sunroof timer function does not work, the input circuit from the ignition switch (IG1) or the driver's door switch, the communication lines between the ETACS-ECU and the sunroof motor assembly (sunroof-ECU), the sunroof motor assembly (sunroof-ECU) or the ETACS-ECU may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the driver’s door switch
  • Malfunction of ETACS-ECU
  • Malfunction of the sunroof motor assembly (sunroof-ECU)
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSTIC PROCEDURE


STEP 1. ECU check on the SWS monitor.


Check that the power supply and earth lines to the sunroof motor assembly (sunroof-ECU) and the ETACS-ECU and the SWS communication lines are normal.

  • Ignition switch: OFF
  • Operate the sunroof switch.
noteOperate within the period set by using the customisation function after the ignition switch is turned off.
ECUS TO BE CHECKED
  • ETACS ECU
  • SUNROOF ECU
OK: "OK" is displayed for all the items

Q. Are the check result normal?

YES ("OK" is displayed for all the items) : Go to Step 2.
NO ("NG" is displayed on the "ETACS ECU" menu.) : Refer to GROUP 54C Inspection Procedure 2 "Communication with the ETACS-ECU is not possible ."
NO ("NG" is displayed on the "SUNROOF ECU" menu.) : Refer to GROUP 54C, Inspection Procedure 5 "Communication with the sunroof motor assembly (sunroof-ECU) is not possible ."
