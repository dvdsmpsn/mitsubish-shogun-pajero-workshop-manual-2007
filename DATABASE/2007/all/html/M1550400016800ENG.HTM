Inspection Procedure 18: The temperature of rear air conditioner cannot be controlled


M15504000168ENG0000010000




CIRCUIT OPERATION


When the temperature adjustment of rear A/C cannot be performed, the circuit between the rear A/C switch and A/C-ECU may have a failure.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the A/C-ECU
  • Malfunction of the rear A/C switch
  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III diagnosis code


Check that the diagnosis code related to the A/C is not set.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Carry out the diagnosis code procedures. Refer to .

STEP 2. Connector check: E-25 rear A/C switch connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the connector.

STEP 3. Check the rear A/C switch.


Refer to .

Q. Is the rear A/C switch in good condition?

Go to Step 4.
Replace the rear A/C switch.

STEP 4. Connector check: D-07 A/C-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the connector.

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness between E-25 rear A/C switch connector (terminals 5, 3 and 13) and D-07 A/C-ECU connector (terminals 47, 41 and 43).








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-219, and junction block connector D-404, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the A/C-ECU input line for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the wiring harness.
