CURTAIN AIR BAG MODULE


M25201000005ENG0000010000




cautionImproper services cause the sudden operation of curtain air bag module or the system to be inoperative. Do not disassemble or tamper with the air bag module to prevent the serious injury.
The curtain air bag module consists of an air bag, an inflator, and the fixing gear relating to those parts, and is installed in the roof side sections (from the driver's and the passenger's front pillars to the rear pillars).
The air bag is made from nylon with the inside coated with silicon, and housed in the roof side sections, folded up compactly.
