HEADLAMP WASHER


M25100000005ENG0000010000


CONSTRUCTION DIAGRAM





  • A headlamp washer mechanism that thrusts out and retracts nozzles, utilizing the water pressure produced by motor has been adopted for some models. This mechanism contributes to better appearances of vehicles when the headlamp washer is not in use. The headlamp washer tank used for this mechanism is also used for the windshield washer.
  • According to the headlamp washer automatic injection function when the headlamp is ON, the headlamp washer operates for the first time only automatically when the windshield washer is turned ON.
