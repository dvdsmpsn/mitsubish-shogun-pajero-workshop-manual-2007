PASSENGER’S AIR BAG OFF INDICATOR LAMP


M25201000016ENG0000010000




The passenger’s air bag off indicator lamp is installed in centre air outlet.
When passenger’s air bag cut off switch is turned OFF, the indicator will stay on to show that the passenger’s (front) air bag is not operational.
When passenger’s air bag cut off switch is turned ON, the indicator goes off to show that the passenger’s (front) air bag is operational.
