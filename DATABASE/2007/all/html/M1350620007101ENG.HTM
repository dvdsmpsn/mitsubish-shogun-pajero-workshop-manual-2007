INSPECTION PROCEDURE 10: ABS operates abnormally.


M13506200071ENG0000010000


COMMENT ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The cause depends on driving and road surface conditions, so diagnosis may be difficult. However, if no diagnosis code is display, carry out the following inspection.

PROBABLE CAUSES


The most likely causes for this case are:

  • Malfunction of the hydraulic brake booster (HBB)

DIAGNOSIS


STEP 1. Check the HBB hydraulic unit.
