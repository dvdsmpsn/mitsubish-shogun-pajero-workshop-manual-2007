Inspection Procedure 3: The Clock Display is not Normal.


M15410800391ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The multi centre display may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of multi centre display
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Replace the multi centre display and retest the system.

Check that the clock display is displayed correctly when the multi centre display is replaced temporarily.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the multi centre display.
Go to Step 2.
STEP 2. Retest the system.

Check that the clock display is displayed on the multi centre display correctly.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Return to Step 1.
