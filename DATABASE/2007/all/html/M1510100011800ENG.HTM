INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT


>>A<< MARK INSTALLATION


1. Installation position
Attach each mark to the position shown in the illustration.






2. Installation procedure
(1)Use 3M ATD Part number 8906 or equivalent to clean the mark installation surfaces on the body.
(2)
cautionWhen attaching the marks, the ambient temperature should be 20 - 38°C and the air should be completely free of dust. If the ambient temperature is lower than 20°C, the marks and the places on the vehicle body where the marks are to be attached should be heated to 20 - 30°C.
Peel off the protection sheet on the back of the marks to paste it on the installation position.
