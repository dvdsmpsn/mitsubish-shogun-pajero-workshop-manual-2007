Inspection Procedure 1: No Navigation Screen is Displayed.


M15410800119ENG0000010000




DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-210 audio visual navigation unit connector




Q. Is D-210 audio visual navigation unit connector in good condition?

Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace the damaged component(s).

STEP 2. Resistance measurement at D-210 audio visual navigation unit connector.




(1)Disconnect audio visual navigation unit connector D-210, and measure the resistance available at the wiring harness side of the connector.

(2)Measure the resistance between terminal 9 and ground.
OK: The resistance should be 2 ohms or less

Q. Is the measured resistance 2 ohms or less?

Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Check the wiring harness between D-210 audio visual navigation unit connector terminal No.9 and ground.





  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 4. Check the wiring harness between D-210 audio visual navigation unit connector terminal No.17 and fusible link (1).





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-241, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply lines (battery supply) for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 5. Connector check: D-208 audio visual navigation unit connector




Q. Is audio visual navigation unit connector D-208 in good condition?

Go to Step 6.
Repair or replace the damaged component(s).

STEP 6. Check the wiring harness between D-208 audio visual navigation unit connector terminal No.21 and fusible link (1).





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-241, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply lines (battery supply) for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 7.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 7. Retest the system


Check if the audio visual navigation unit power is turned ON.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the audio visual navigation unit.
