Inspection Procedure 20: No Sound. (CD Only)


M15410800326ENG0000010000


DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check again using a normal CD, which is not dirty or scratched.

Q. When you substitute another normal CD, is the CD played normally?

The original CD is defective. The CD player should sound normally.
Go to Step 2.
STEP 2. Check power supply to the CD player when the ignition switch is at "ACC" or "ACC".

Q. Is the radio and CD player energized when the ignition switch is turned to the "ACC" or "ON" position?

Replace the radio and CD player. The procedure is complete.
Check the memory backup power supply circuit. Refer to Inspection Procedure 1 .
