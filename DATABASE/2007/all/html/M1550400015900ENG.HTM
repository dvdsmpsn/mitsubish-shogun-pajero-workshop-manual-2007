Inspection Procedure 20: Malfunction of the A/C-ECU power supply system


M15504000159ENG0000010000




CIRCUIT OPERATION


If a power supply is not provided to the A/C-ECU, the A/C-ECU power supply system or the earth system may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the A/C-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-08 A/C-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Voltage measurement at the D-08 A/C-ECU connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the ON position.

(3)measure the voltage between terminal 20 and body earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Check the wiring harness between D-08 A/C-ECU connector terminal No.20 and the ignition switch (IG2).






notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check junction block connector D-403 and D-401, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the blower relay power supply line for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction .)
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 4. Voltage measurement at D-08 A/C-ECU connector.




(1)Remove the relay, and measure at the junction block side.

(2)Voltage between terminal 19 and body earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Go to Step 5.

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness between D-08 A/C-ECU connector terminal No.19 and fusible link (1).








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-241 and joint connector D-34, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the A/C-ECU power supply line for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction .)
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Resistance measurement at the D-08 A/C-ECU connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.

(2)Continuity between terminal 9 and body earth
OK: Continuity (Less than 2 ohms)

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the A/C-ECU.
Go to Step 7.

STEP 7. Check the wiring harness between D-08 A/C-ECU connector terminal No.9 and body earth.





  • Check the A/C-ECU earth line for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the wiring harness.
