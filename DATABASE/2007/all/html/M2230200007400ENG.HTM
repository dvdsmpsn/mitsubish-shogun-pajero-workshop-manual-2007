CONSTRUCTION DIAGRAM

The transmission oil cooler is a water-cooled type which is built into the radiator. This water cooled-type transmission oil cooler has an aluminium plate-type (layered type) construction in contrast to the previous concentric-type (cylindrical) oil cooler. This new design has been adopted in order to improve cooling performance.
