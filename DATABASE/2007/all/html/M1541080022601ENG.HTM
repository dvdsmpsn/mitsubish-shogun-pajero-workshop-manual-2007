Inspection Procedure 1: The Rear Display does not Display at all.


M15410800226ENG0000010000


DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: E-129 rear display unit connector



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 2. Voltage measurement at the E-129 rear display unit connector.



(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness-side connector.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the ACC position.

(3)Voltage between the E-129 rear display unit connector terminal No.4 and the body earth
OK: Battery voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Wiring harness check between the E-129 rear display unit connector terminal No.4 and ignition swtich (ACC).





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-241 and E-131, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply lines (ACC power supply) for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 4. Voltage measurement at E-129 rear display unit connector.



(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the rear display unit side connector.
(2)Voltage between the E-129 rear display unit connector terminal No.3 and the body earth
OK: Battery voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Go to Step 5.

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness wires between E-129 rear display unit connector terminal No.3 and fusible link (1).





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-241 and E-131, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply line for open circuit and short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Resistance measurement at the E-129 rear display unit connector



(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.

(2)Measure the resistance between E-129 rear display unit connector terminal No.1 and the body earth.
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less)

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Go to Step 7.

STEP 7. Wiring harness check between E-129 rear display unit connector terminal No.1 and the body earth




  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.


notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-131, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 8. Retest the system


Check if the rear display unit displays normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the rear display unit.
