Code No. B1041,B1042: Potentiometer system for the front air mix damper


M15504000127ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


This code is set when the air mixing damper control motor potentiometer does not send any signal to the A/C-ECU due to short or open circuit.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the front air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer
  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors
  • Malfunction of the automatic A/C control panel (A/C-ECU)

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-08 A/C-ECU connector and D-201 front air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer connector






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Check the wiring harness between D-08 A/C-ECU connector (terminals 23, 30 and 8) and D-201 front air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer connector (terminals 3, 7 and 5).







  • Check the potentiometer power supply, earth and signal line for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 3. Check the front air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Replace the front air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer.
