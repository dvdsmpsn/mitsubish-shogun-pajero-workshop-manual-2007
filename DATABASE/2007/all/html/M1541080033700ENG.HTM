Rear view camera image is not displayed normally.


M15410800337ENG0000010000


DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the MMCS navigation screen.


Check whether the MMCS navigation screen is displayed normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Diagnose the MMCS. Refer to .

STEP 2. Check the communication and wire connection in the MMCS service mode.


Perform "Network/Connect Line Check" in the MMCS service mode to check that the communication and wire connection with the rear view camera* are OK. Refer to .
note*: Is displayed as "Rear Camera" in the service mode.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Connector check: G-10 rear view camera connector, D-205 audio visual navigation unit connector





Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the connector concerned.

STEP 4. Check the harness wire between G-10 rear view camera connector terminal Nos.1, 3, 4, 5, and 6 and D-205 audio visual navigation unit connector terminal Nos.83, 81, 85, 86, and 82.






  • Check the communication lines for open circuit.


notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-221 and E-108, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 9.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 5. Perform "Vehicle Signal Check" in the MMCS service mode.


Perform "Vehicle Signal Check" in the MMCS service mode to check whether "Shift Position R" signal is normal when the selector lever <M/T> or shift lever <A/T> is "R" position. (Refer to .)

Q. Is the "Shift Position R" signal transmitted normally?

Go to Step 9.
Go to Step 6.

STEP 6. Check the rear wiper.


Check if the rear wiper operates as specified when the ignition switch is in the "ACC" or "ON" position and the shift lever <M/T> or selector lever <A/T> is set to the "R" position.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 7.
Refer to Group 51B - Troubleshooting .

STEP 7. Connector check: D-208 audio visual navigation unit connector, D-405 ETACS-ECU connector






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 8. Check the harness wire between D-208 audio visual navigation unit connector terminal No.9 and D-405 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.2.







  • Check the communication lines for open circuit.


notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-221 and E-108, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 9. Substitute a known good rear view camera, and check the trouble symptom.


Check whether image sent from the rear view camera is displayed on the display of MMCS normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the rear view camera.
Replace the audio visual navigation unit.

STEP 10. Substitute a known good audio visual navigation unit, and check the trouble symptom.


Check whether image sent from the rear view camera is displayed on the display of MMCS normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the audio visual navigation unit.
Replace the ETACS-ECU.
