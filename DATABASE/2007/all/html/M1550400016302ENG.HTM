Inspection Procedure 15: The rear blower does not work. <Dual A/C>


M15504000163ENG0000010001




CIRCUIT OPERATION


If the rear blower motor does not operate, the rear blower motor circuit system may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the A/C-ECU
  • Malfunction of the rear blower relay
  • Malfunction of the rear A/C switch
  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors
  • Malfunction of the rear blower motor

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check that the blower motor operates when the blower switch is moved to the "3 (HI) " position.


(1)Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.
(2)Turn the rear blower switch to the "3 (HI) " position.
