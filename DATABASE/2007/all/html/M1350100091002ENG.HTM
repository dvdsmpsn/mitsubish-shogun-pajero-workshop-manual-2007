caution
  • Do not turn the ignition switch ON before the brake fluid has been refilled to prevent the HBB pump motor from damage.
  • When handling the HBB, hold the stud bolt or the aluminum body at the front or rear end.
  • To avoid water ingress, do not touch the lead wires or the lead wire grommet.
  • Use the valve spring compressor adapter (MB991620) to hold the HBB, otherwise its component will be deformed or damaged due to its own weight.
  • Do not subject he component to any shocks or drop. If it is dropped, always replace it.
  • Be careful not to spill brake fluid. If any is spilt, it should be wiped off with a cloth immediately.
  • Do not apply water to the HBB.
