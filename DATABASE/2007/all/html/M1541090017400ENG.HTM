Inspection Procedure D-7: The Foot Lamps and Ceiling Lamp do not Illuminate or Extinguish Normally.


M15410900174ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The ETACS-ECU operates the foot lamps and ceiling lamp in accordance with the input signals below.

  • Ignition switch (IG1)
  • Door switches
  • Back door switch
  • Driver's door lock actuator switch (unlock switch)
  • Interior lamp switch
If this function does not work normally, these input signal circuit(s) or the ETACS-ECU may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the door switches
  • Malfunction of the room lamp switches
  • Malfunction of the driver's door lock actuator switch (unlock switch)
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Pulse check


Check the input signals below, which are related to the foot lamp and ceiling lamp.
System switch
Check condition
Driver's door switch
Driver's door is opened while all the other doors are closed.
Passenger’s door switch
Passenger's door is opened while all the other doors are closed.
Rear door (RH) switch
Rear door (RH) is opened while all the other doors are closed
Rear door (LH) switch
Rear door (LH) door is opened while all the other doors are closed
Back door switch
Back door is opened while all the other doors are closed
Driver's door lock actuator switch (unlock switch)
When the driver's door is unlocked or locked
Interior lamp ON switch
When turned from OFF to ON
Interior lamp OFF switch
When turned from ON to OFF
OK: The M.U.T.-III sounds or the voltmeter needle fluctuates.

Q. Are the check result normal?

YES (All the signals are received normally) : Go to Step 2.
NO (All the door switch signals are not received) : Refer to GROUP 54Ad - Input Signal Chart .
NO (The driver's door lock actuator switch (unlock switch) signal is not received) : Refer to GROUP 54Ad - Input Signal Chart .
NO (The interior lamp switch signal is not received) : Refer to GROUP 54Ad - Input Signal Chart .

STEP 2. Retest the system.


Check that the foot lamp and ceiling lamp illuminates when the interior lamp switch is operated.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Check the illumination of the foot lamps and the ceiling lamp.



  • Check that the foot lamps and ceiling lamp light up/off when the interior lamp switch is turned to the ON/OFF position.

Q. Which of the foot lamps or the ceiling lamp does no illuminate or extinguish?

YES (All of the lamps illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (The front foot lamp (RH or LH) does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 4.
NO (The rear foot lamp does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 7.
NO (The ceiling lamp does not illuminate normally) : Go to Step 11.
