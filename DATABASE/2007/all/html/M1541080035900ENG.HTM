ADJUSTMENT OF VOLUME AND SOUND QUALITY AUTOMATIC CORRECTION FUNCTION


M15410800359ENG0000010000



When the following operations are performed with the audio power ON, the sound volume during driving and the ON/OFF of sound quality automatic correction function are switched.

  1. Press and hold (approximately 2 seconds) the sound adjustment switch.
  2. SCV ON (when the automatic correction function is ON) or SCV OFF (when the automatic correction function is OFF) is displayed on the multi centre display.
  3. Turn the sound adjustment switch to switch between SCV ON and OFF.
  4. Press the sound adjustment switch or leave as it is for 10 seconds or more.
  5. Go back to the audio normal screen on the multi centre display.
