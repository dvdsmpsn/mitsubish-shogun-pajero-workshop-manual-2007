Inspection Procedure D-2: The Room Lamp does not Illuminate Normally. <Vehicles with Keyless Entry System>


M15410900124ENG0000010001


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the room lamp does not illuminate or extinguish normally, wiring harness connector(s) or the bulb may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the interior lamp switches
  • Malfunction of the room lamp bulbs
  • Malfunction of the rear personal lamp bulbs
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

STEP 4. Connector check: D-419 ETACS-ECU connector, E-102 room lamp connector

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.6 to E-102 room lamp connector terminal No.10.







notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

Replace the room lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Check the room lamp switch.


Check which position (ON or DOOR) of the room lamp switch does not activate the room lamp.

Q. Which switch position does not activate the lamp?

YES (The lamp illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (The lamp does not illuminates at neither the ON nor DOOR position) : Go to Step 7.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the ON position) : Go to Step 7.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the DOOR position) : Go to Step 9.
