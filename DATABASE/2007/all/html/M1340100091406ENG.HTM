REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> LOWER ARM MOUNTING BOLT REMOVAL



1. Using the floor jack, compress the coil spring to remove the lower arm mounting bolt.
2. Lower the floor jack slowly, and then remove the coil spring.

<<B>> ADJUSTING BOLT/ADJUSTING PLATE (FOR CAMBER ADJUSTMENT) REMOVAL



Make the mating marks to the rear suspension frame, adusting plate and adjusting bolt, and then remove the adjusting bolt and adjusting plate.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT


>>A<< COIL SPRING/LOWER ARM MOUNTING BOLT INSTALLATION


1. The identification color of the coil spring should be located at the lower side.
2. Align the coil spring end with the spring lower pad cavity.

3. Place a piece of wood at the lower arm as shown in the illustration. Using the floor jack, compress the coil spring to remove the lower arm mounting bolt.
