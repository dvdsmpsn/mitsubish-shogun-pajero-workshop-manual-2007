REMOVAL SERVICE POINT


<<A>> ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR REMOVAL



Use special tool mater temperature sensor wrench (MB992042) to remove the engine coolant temperature sensor.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< O-RING INSTALLATION


caution
  • Care must be taken not to permit engine oil or other greases to adhere to the O-ring.
  • When inserting the pipe, check to be sure that there is no sand, dirt, etc. on its inner surface.

Fit the O-ring to the groove in the cooling water outlet pipe and water pump inlet pipe. Then lubricate the O-ring and the inside of the cooling water outlet fitting and water pump assembly with water, and then insert the cooling water outlet pipe and water pump inlet pipe to the cooling water outlet fitting and water pump assembly.

>>B<< ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR INSTALLATION



1. Apply the specified sealant to the thread of the engine coolant temperature sensor.
Specified Sealant: 3M™ AAD Part No. 8731 or equivalent

2. Use special tool mater temperature sensor wrench (MB992042) to install the engine coolant temperature sensor.
