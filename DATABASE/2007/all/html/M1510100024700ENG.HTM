REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> RIVET REMOVAL


Refer to the Disassembly Service Point for FRONT BUMPER ().

<<B>> FRONT/REAR OVERFENDER REMOVAL



Use the special tool ornament remover (MB990784) to pry out the overfender.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< RIVET INSTALLATION


Refer to the Reassembly Service Point for FRONT BUMPER ().

>>B<< FRONT/REAR OVERFENDER PAD INSTALLATION


1. Wipe the double-sided tape application surface on the front/rear overfender and clean it with a rag moistened with isopropyl alcohol.
2. Tear off the double-sided tape backing paper of the front/rear overfender pad.
3. Install the pad to the overfender.
note If the double-sided tape is difficult to affix in cold temperature, etc., warm the bonding surfaces of the overfender to about 40 - 60°C before affixing the tape.
4. Firmly press in the pad to the overfender.

>>C<< PROTECT FILM INSTALLATION



1. INSTALLATION POSITION
2.
caution
  • The ambient temperature should be 20 to 38°C. Ensure that the working area is clean.
  • If ambient temperature is less than 20°C, heat the protect film and application surface to a temperature of 20 to 30°C.
  • Be careful that air bubbles are not formed under the protect film.
INSTALLATION PROCEDURE
(1)Wipe the protect film application surface and clean it with a rag moistened with isopropyl alcohol.
(2)Remove backing paper from the protect film, and apply the film to the specified position.
