NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



1. Cut the side outer panel as shown in the illustration to separate the weld points of the centre pillar reinforcement and side sill reinforcement at the side sill section.

2. When cutting the centre pillar upper, the roof side inner rail is positioned in the cut area, and thus cut the roof side inner rail as shown in the figure, and then lift it upward.

3. After removing the centre pillar, remove the centre pillar seat belt reinforcement remaining on the body side.

INSTALLATION



1. To reinforce the strength of the centre pillar upper area that was cut, cut the side outer panel 20 mm above the cut area of the centre pillar top part.

2.
cautionWeld and repair if the centre pillar seat belt reinforcement is damaged.
Cut only the centre pillar reinforcement, aligned with the body-side, of the new centre pillar reinforcement parts so the centre pillar seat belt reinforcement is not damaged.

3. When assembling the new centre pillar outer part, apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

4. When returning the lifted roof side rail inner, apply the adhesive to the area on the body side as shown in the figure. Also, weld the cut area with MIG arc welding (continuous).
