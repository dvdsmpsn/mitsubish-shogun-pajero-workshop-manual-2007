The instrument panel offers the following features:

  • The slush formed soft covering material has been adopted to emphasise the luxurious feel.
  • The structure which conceals the air bag separation line under the surface has been adopted to improve appearance and quality.
  • Clearances and gaps of parts have been minimised to emphasise the accurate superior feel.
  • The unique audio system has been adopted for the solid-type instrument panel to emphasise the luxurious and superior feel.
  • The aluminium design has been adopted for the ornament panel, shift lever area, cup holder and so on to improve the appearance.
  • The soft leather has been adopted for the armrest to emphasise the luxurious feel.
  • The glove box with a key has been adopted.
  • The two-step glove box has been adopted for convenience.
  • The lamp has been installed to the inside of the glove box for convenience at night-time.
  • The card holder (can hold approx. 3 cards) and centre box have been installed to the centre bottom for convenience.
  • The lid which can be closed when the card holder (can hold approx. 3 cards) and centre box are not used has been adopted for better appearance.
  • The armrest box has been installed to the top of the rear floor console and the rear floor console box to the bottom, to ensure storage spaces.
  • The cup holder has been installed to the top of the floor console, and the tray in front of the holder.
  • The movable ashtray, which can be mounted to the cup holder on top of the floor console or to the armrest of rear seat has been installed to improve convenience. <Option>
