SUNROOF FIT ADJUSTMENT


M14206000010ENG0000010000


1. Close the sunroof lid glass fully.
2. Open the rear sun shade fully.

3. Loosen the sunroof lid glass assembly mounting screws. Adjust the sunroof lid glass height by moving the glass assembly along the guide oblong hole so that the clearance between the glass and the vehicle body is even.
4. After adjustment, check that the sunroof operates smoothly.
