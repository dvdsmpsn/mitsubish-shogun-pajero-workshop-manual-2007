LOWER ARM BALL JOINT DUST COVER CHECK


M13302000088ENG0000010000


1. Check the lower arm ball joint dust cover for cracks or damage by pushing it with your finger.
2. If the lower arm ball joint dust cover is cracked or damaged, replace the lower arm ball joint assembly.
noteIf the dust cover is cracked or damaged, it is possible that there may also be damage to the ball joint.
