PAD WEAR CHECK


caution
  • Always replace the brake pads as an axle set.
  • If an excessive difference is found in the thickness between the right and left brake pads, check moving parts.

Measure thickness at the thinnest and worn area of the pad. Replace the pad assembly if the pad thickness is less than the limit value.
Standard value: 10.0 mm
Minimum limit: 2.0 mm
