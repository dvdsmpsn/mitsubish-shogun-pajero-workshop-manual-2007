Inspection Procedure 5: Engine Coolant Temperature Gauge does not Work (other meters work).


M15410100033ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If only the engine coolant temperature gauge does not operate, the engine-ECU <M/T> or engine-A/T-ECU <A/T> system, the combination meter, the wiring harness or its connector may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Malfunction of engine-ECU <M/T> or engine-A/T-ECU <A/T>
  • Malfunction of combination meter

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics


Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the CAN bus lines (Refer to GROUP 54D - Troubleshooting ). After diagnosing the CAN bus lines, go to Step 5.

STEP 2. M.U.T.-III other system diagnosis code


Check whether the MPI system or Diesel system-related diagnosis code is set.

Q. Is the diagnosis code set?

Diagnose the MPI system or Diesel system (Refer to GROUP 13Ab, Troubleshooting <MPI system> or GROUP 13Bb, Troubleshooting <Diesel system>). After diagnosing the MPI system or Diesel system, go to Step 5.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. M.U.T.-III data list



  • Item No.8: Water temperature gauge (Refer to .)

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

Go to Step 4.
Diagnose the MPI system or Diesel system (Refer to GROUP 13Ab, Troubleshooting <MPI system> or GROUP 13Bb, Troubleshooting <Diesel system>). After diagnosing the MPI system or Diesel system, go to Step 5.

STEP 4. M.U.T.-III actuator test



  • Item No.4: Water temperature gauge
OK: The water thermometer operates.

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

Go to Step 5.
Replace combination meter and then go to step 5.

STEP 5. Retest the system.


Check to see that the engine coolant temperature gauge operates normally.

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

The procedure is complete.
Go to Step 1.
