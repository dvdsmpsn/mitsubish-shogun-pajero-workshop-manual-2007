MIVEC (Mitsubishi Innovative Valve Electronic Control System)


M21302000235ENG0000010000


Engine-A/T-ECU turns the oil feeder control valve ON/OFF according to engine speed in order to control oil pressure acting on the piston in the rocker arm. Thus, change is done between low-speed cam and high-speed cam.
System Configuration Diagram

Engine-A/T-ECU turns OFF (Duty: 0 %) the oil feeder control valve at low engine speed (3,000 r/min or less). As a result, oil pressure does not act on the piston in the rocker arm and inlet valve is driven by the low speed cam. Engine-A/T-ECU turns ON (Duty: For 2 seconds after change 100 %; after 2 seconds 60 %) the oil feeder control valve at high engine speed (3,000 r/min or more). As a result, oil pressure acts on the piston in the rocker arm and inlet valve is driven by the high speed cam. It will be continually driven by the low speed cam under following conditions.

  • Engine coolant temperature is less than 20°C.
  • For 10 seconds after engine is fully started.
