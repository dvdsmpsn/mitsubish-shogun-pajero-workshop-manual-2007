D5. CHECK BRAKE SHOE LININGS AND DISCS FOR WEAR


M60200000051ENG0000010000


BRAKE LINING THICKNESS CHECK


1. Remove the brake disc.

2. Measure the thickness of the brake lining at several places.
Standard value:

    <Short wheelbase> 3.0 mm
    <Long wheelbase> 4.0 mm
Limit: 1.0 mm
3. If the thickness of the brake lining is below the limit, replace the shoe and lining assemblies on both sides of the vehicle. Never replace only one side.

BRAKE DISC INSIDE DIAMETER CHECK


1. Remove the brake disc.

2. Measure the inside diameter of the brake disc in two places or more.
Standard value:

    <Short wheelbase> 199.0 mm
    <Long wheelbase> 210.0 mm
Limit:

    <Short wheelbase> 200.0 mm
    <Long wheelbase> 211.0 mm
3. If the inside diameter exceeds the limit, of if it is excessively worn or one side, replace the brake disc.
