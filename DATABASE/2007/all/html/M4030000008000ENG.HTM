NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



1. When removing the quarter outer extension upper, to secure the strength of the rear pillar cut area, cut the quarter outer extension upper 50 mm below the side outer panel cut area.

2. When removing only the quarter outer extension lower (LH), the quarter outer extension upper (LH) interferes with it, and thus cut the area of the quarter outer extension lower (LH) in advance as shown in the figure, and bend it inward.
3. When replacing only the rear lamp housing (RH), the quarter outer extension lower (RH) interferes with it, and thus remove the quarter outer extension lower (RH) in advance.

INSTALLATION



1. When installing the new rear lamp housing (RH) part, apply the body sealant in advance to the area as shown in the figure.

2. When installing the new quarter outer extension lower parts, apply the adhesive in advance to the area as shown in the figure.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

3. When installing the rear quarter outer panel, apply the body sealant in advance to the areas as shown in the figure.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

4. Reference
Partial replacement is possible depending on the range of damage.
