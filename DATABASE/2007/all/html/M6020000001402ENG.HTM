A10. CHECK AIR CLEANER ELEMENT FOR CLOGGING AND DAMAGE


M60200000014ENG0000010000



1. Check air cleaner element for clogging and damage.
2. Clean deposited dust from the element in the following manner.
(1)Lightly tap the element against the top of a bench.
(2)Blow compressed air from inside the element.
3. Wipe off dust on the air cleaner interior.
4. Install the air cleaner body.
