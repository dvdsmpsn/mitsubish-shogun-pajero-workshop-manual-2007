EVACUATION OF WATER FROM FUEL FILTER


M11303000012ENG0000010000


Water is in the fuel filter cartridge when fuel filter indicator lamp illuminates. Evacuate water by the following procedures.

1. Loosen the drain plug.
2. After water is evacuated by using an hand pump, tighten the drain plug to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 1.0 ± 0.4 N·m
