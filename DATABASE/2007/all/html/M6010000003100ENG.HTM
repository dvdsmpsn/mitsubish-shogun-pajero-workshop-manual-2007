24. OPERATION OF SERVICE BRAKES AND PARKING BRAKES

1. Service brakes
(1)Check the clearance between the brake pedal and the floorboard when the brake pedal is depressed.
(2)Verify correct brake pedal free play.
noteFor inspection and adjustment of the service brake, refer to GROUP 2, Periodic Inspection and Maintenance.
2. Parking brake
Check the parking brake drag and lever travel.
noteFor inspection and adjustment of the parking brake, refer to GROUP 2, Periodic Inspection and Maintenance.
