NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



When removing the quarter panel inner, remove it with the rear pillar reinforcement corner.

INSTALLATION



1. When installing the quarter panel inner, remove the rear seat belt reinforcement upper from the new part, assemble the rear pillar reinforcement corner to the rear seat belt reinforcement upper, and then install it to the quarter panel inner.


2. When installing the quarter panel inner, apply the structural adhesive in advance to the areas of the quarter panel inner centre pillar and rear pillar as shown in the figure.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

3. When installing the quarter panel inner, apply the body sealant in advance to the area of the quarter panel inner as shown in the figure.

4. Because it is difficult to apply the adhesive to the roof rail, weld with MIG plug welding at 4 spots.

5. Assemble the quarter panel inner, then cover the hole and flange with bolt and aluminium tape and fill the hole with foam materials as shown in the figure of the instructions.
Foam: 3M ATD ULTRAPRO Panel foam-Yellow

6. Wait 2 hours after filling the foam materials to remove the bolt and aluminium tape. Then melt the foam materials with a soldering gun so a clip, etc. can thoroughly be inserted in the hole that was clogged with foam materials, to bore open the hole.
