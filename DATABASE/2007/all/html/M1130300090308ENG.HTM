No. 2 INTAKE AIR TEMPERATURE SENSOR CHECK


M11303000903ENG0000010000



1. Disconnect the No. 2 intake air temperature sensor connector.
2. Measure resistance between terminals No. 1 and No. 2.
Standard value:
    13 - 18 kΩ (at -20°C)
    5.1 - 6.9 kΩ (at 0°C)
    2.0 - 3.0 kΩ (at 20°C)
    0.9 - 1.5 kΩ (at 40°C)
    0.40 - 0.78 kΩ (at 60°C)
    0.23 - 0.42 kΩ (at 80°C)
3. Remove the No. 2 intake air temperature sensor

4. Measure resistance while heating the sensor using a hair drier.
Normal condition:

5. If the value deviates from the standard value or the resistance remains unchanged, replace the No. 2 intake air temperature sensor sensor assembly.
6. Install the air flow sensor and tighten it to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 13.5 ± 1.5 N·m
