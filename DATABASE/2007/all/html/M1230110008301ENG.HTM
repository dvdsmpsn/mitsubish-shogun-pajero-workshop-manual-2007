Inspection Procedure 17: 4LLc detection switch system


M12301100083ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • System voltage is applied to the 4LLc detection switch output terminal (terminal No.1) from the engine-A/T-ECU (terminal No.9) via the resistance in the unit.
  • The 4LLc detection switch is grounded through the transfer case to the vehicle body.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the transmission shifts to 4th gear when the transfer lever is in the 4LLc position, the cause is probably a malfunction of the 4LLc detection switch.
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the 4LLc detection switch
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Malfunction of the engine-A/T-ECU
