REMOVAL SERVICE POINT


<<A>> AXLE NUT REMOVAL


cautionDo not apply pressure to wheel bearing by the vehicle weight to avoid possible damage to wheel bearing before tightening drive shaft nut fully.

Use special tool front hub & flange yoke holder (MB990767) to fix hub and remove the axle nut.

<<B>> REAR DRIVESHAFT ASSEMBLY REMOVAL



Fit the following special tools in the groove to pull out the rear driveshaft assembly.

  • Sliding hammer (MB990211)
  • Driveshaft puller (MB992204)

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< AXLE NUT INSTALLATION



1. Using special tool front hub and flange yoke holder (MB990767), tighten the driveshaft nut.
cautionDo not apply pressure to wheel bearing by the vehicle weight to avoid possible damage when axle nut is loosened.

>>B<< AXLE CAP INSTALLATION



1. If axle cap is as shown after it is fitted, turn it at least one notch clockwise to align it with the split pin hole.

2. If the axle cap is as shown, turn it at least one notch anticlockwise to align it with the split pin hole.
3. If the split pin hole is not visible, turn it at least three notches clockwise to align it with the split pin hole.
