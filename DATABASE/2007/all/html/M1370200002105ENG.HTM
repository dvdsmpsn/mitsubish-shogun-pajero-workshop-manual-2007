FLUID REPLACEMENT


M13702000021ENG0000010000


1. Raise and support the front wheels.

2. Disconnect the return hose connection, and then connect a vinyl hose to the return hose, and drain the fluid into a container.
3. While operating the starter motor intermittently, turn the steering wheel all the way to the left and right several times to drain all of the fluid.
4. Connect the return hose securely, and then secure with the clip.
5. Fill the oil reservoir with specified fluid up to the lower position of the filler, and then bleed the air.
Specified fluid: ATF DEXRON III or DEXRON II
