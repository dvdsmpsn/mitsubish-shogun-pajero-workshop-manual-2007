When the ignition key is at a position other than the LOCK (OFF) position, the selector lever can be moved from the P to the R position even though the brake pedal is not depressed.
1

When the ignition key is at a position other than the LOCK (OFF) position, the selector lever cannot be moved from the P to the R position even though the brake pedal is being depressed.
2

The ignition key is at the LOCK (OFF) position, but the selector lever can be moved from the P to the R position when the brake pedal is depressed.
3

The selector lever cannot be easily moved from the P to the R position.
4

The selector lever cannot be moved from the R to the P position.
5

The ignition key cannot be turned to the LOCK (OFF) position when the selector lever is at the P position.
6

The ignition key can be turned to the LOCK (OFF) position when the selector lever is at a position other than the P position.
