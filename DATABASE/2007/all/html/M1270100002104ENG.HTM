HUB STARTING TORQUE CHECK



1. Tighten the following special tools in rear hub assembly to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 220 ± 10 N·m

  • Bolt (MB990999)
  • Drag link bushing remover and installer A (MB990843)

2. Measure the hub starting torque by using the following special tools.

  • Torque wrench (MB990685)
  • Preload socket (MB990326)
Limit: 3.0 N·m or less
3. Hub starting torque must be under the limit value and there should be no roughness when rotating the hub.

WHEEL BEARING AXIAL PLAY CHECK




1. Measure to determine whether the wheel bearing axial play is within the limit or not by using the following special tools.

  • Bolt (MB990999)
  • Drag link bushing remover and installer A (MB990843)
Limit: 0 mm
2. If the wheel bearing axial play exceeds the limit value at the specified torque of 220 ± 10 N·m, replace the rear hub assembly.
