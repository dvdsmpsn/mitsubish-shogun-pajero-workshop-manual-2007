NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



1. When partially replacing the rear sidemember, the rear floor sidemember reinforcement B interferes with the cut area, and thus remove the floor sidemember reinforcement B in advance.

2. For the partial replacement of the rear sidemember, cut the part 105 mm rearward from the tip of rear floor crossmember extension.

INSTALLATION



1. When assembling the new quarter inner extension lower parts, apply in advance body sealant to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.

2. When assembling the new rear floor pan parts, apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent
