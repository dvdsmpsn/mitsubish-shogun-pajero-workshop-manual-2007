FUEL TANK

The fuel tank assembly consists of the following components:

  • Fuel tank
  • Fuel tank pump assembly
  • Fuel tank gauge unit
  • Valve assembly
  • Fuel high pressure tube
  • Under protector
The fuel tank pump assembly consists of the following components:

  • Fuel pump assembly
  • Fuel tank filter
  • Fuel tank pump pressure regulator
As for the fuel tank, a steel fuel tank which is located underneath the passenger seat floor has been adopted in the same way as the conventional PAJERO. It has the following features.

  • Lead-free materials have been adopted for fuel tank in consideration of protection of the global environment.
  • A plastic under protector which is light in weight and has excellent corrosion resistance has been adopted.
  • A valve assembly with an integrated cut-off valve which prevents fuel outflow and two-way valve which adjusts the fuel pressure inside the fuel tank has been adopted.
