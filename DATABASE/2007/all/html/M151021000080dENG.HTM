Inspection Procedure 1: The windshield wipers does not work at all.


M15102100008ENG0000010001


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The windshield wiper motor, the column switch or the front-ECU may be defective. If a problem is found in the ECU check by using the SWS monitor, also check the wiper backup circuit (between D-311 column switch connector terminal No.8 and A-09X front-ECU connector terminal No.26) and repair if necessary.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the windshield wiper motor
  • Malfunction of the column switch
  • Malfunction of the front-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


Step 1. ECU check by using the SWS monitor.


Check that the power supply and earth lines to the column switch (column-ECU) and the front-ECU and the SWS communication lines are normal.

  • Turn the ignition switch to the ON position.
ECUS TO BE CHECKED
  • COLUMN ECU
  • FRONT-ECU
OK: "OK" is displayed for all the items

Q. Are the check result normal?

"OK" is displayed for all the items : Go to Step 2.
"NG" is displayed on the "COLUMN ECU" menu. : Refer to GROUP 54C - SWS, Inspection Procedure 1 "Communication with the column switch (column-ECU) is not possible ."
"NG" is displayed on the "FRONT ECU" menu. : Refer to GROUP 54C - SWS, Inspection Procedure 3 "Communication with the front-ECU is not possible ."

Step 2. Function diagnosis by using the SWS monitor.


Check the SWS communication signal, which are related to the windshield wiper function.

(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.

(2)Continuity between B-09 windshield wiper motor connector terminal No.5, and body earth

Go to Step 7.
Go to Step 6.

notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check the intermediate connector A-05 and repair if necessary.

Step 8. Voltage measurement at the A-09X front-ECU connector.



(1)Remove the front-ECU, and measure at the relay box side.
(2)Ignition switch: ACC

(3)Check the voltage between the A-09X front-ECU connector terminal No.24 and body earth.
OK: Battery voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 9.

Step 9. Check the wiring harness between A-09X front-ECU connector terminal No.24 and the ignition switch (ACC).





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check the intermediate connector D-238 and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply line to the ignition switch (ACC) for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10.
Repair the wiring harness.

Step 10. Retest the system.


The windshield wiper should now work normally at all.

Q. Is the check result normal?
