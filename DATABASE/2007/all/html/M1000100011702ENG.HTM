INITIALIZATION PROCEDURE FOR LEARNING VALUE IN MPI ENGINE <6G7>


M10001000117ENG0000010000


Initialization Procedure


1. After the ignition switch is in "LOCK" (OFF) position, connect M.U.T.-III with the diagnosis connector.
2. Select the item on the screen of the initialization for learning, and perform the initialization.
