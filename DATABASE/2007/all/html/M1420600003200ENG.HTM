SUNROOF LID GLASS OPERATION CURRENT CHECK


M14206000032ENG0000010000



1. Remove the sunroof fuse from the fuse box, and then connect a circuit tester as shown.
2. Operate the sunroof switch to open the sunroof. Measure when the sunroof is half opened.
Standard value: 7 A or less (at 20 °C)
3. Check the following areas if the operating current of the sunroof lid glass exceeds the standard value.

  • Improperly installed sunroof assembly, deformation or any foreign substances
  • Drive cable fastening
  • Tilting of sunroof lid glass
