PASSENGER'S AIR BAG OFF INDICATOR LAMP CHECK


M15204000986ENG0000010000



1. Check that the passenger’s air bag OFF indicator lamp illuminates when the ignition switch is in the "ON" position.
2. Check that the passenger’s air bag off indicator lamp illuminates for about 7 seconds and extinguishes. (The lamp does not extinguish when the passenger's air bag cut off switch is air bag OFF position.)
3. If not, check for diagnosis code.
