MANIFOLD VACUUM CHECK


M11102000904ENG0000010000


1. Before inspection, set the vehicle to the pre-inspection condition.

2. Turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position and then connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.

3. Disconnect the ventilation hose from the positive crankcase ventilation (PVC) valve, and then connect a vacuum gauge to the ventilation hose. Plug the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve.
4. Start the engine and check that idle speed is within the standard valve.
Standard value: 700 ± 100 r/min
5. Check the inlet manifold vacuum.
Limit: Minimum 65 kPa
6. Turn off the ignition switch.
7. Remove the vacuum gauge and the three-way joint, and then connect the vacuum hose.
8. Remove the engine tachometer or the M.U.T.-III.
