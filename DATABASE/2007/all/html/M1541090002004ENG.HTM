REMOVAL SERVICE POINT


<<A>> HEADLAMP BULB REMOVAL <VEHICLES WITH DISCHARGE HEADLAMP>


cautionDo not touch the bulb surface with bare hands or dirty gloves. If the bulb surface (glass part) gets dirty, immediately clean it with alcohol or thinner. After drying completely, install the bulb.

1. Twist the headlamp bulb socket cover to remove.

2. Disconnect the connector.
3. Turn the igniter to remove.
4. Release the bulb securing spring, and remove the bulb.
