The following two types of speakers are available.



  • 6 speakers

    • Tweeter: 3.5 cm
    • Front door: 16 cm
    • Rear door or rear: 16 cm



  • 12 speakers <ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>

    • Front and rear tweeters: 3.5-cm soft dome tweeter
    • Front door and rear door: 16 cm Mid-Bass speaker
    • Instrument panel: Mid-range 8-cm speaker
    • Back door: 8 cm Mid-range speaker
    • Subwoofer: 25-cm dual voice coiled

TWEETER





  • The tweeters are incorporated in the delta cover trim.



  • For vehicles with 12 speakers, soft dome tweeters are installed in the delta covers and the rear doors. The soft dome tweeter can play clearer treble with less distortion compared to the balance dome tweeter. <ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>

SPEAKER






  • 16-cm speakers are installed on the front and rear doors.
  • For vehicles with 12 speakers, 16-cm mid-bass speakers are installed on the front and rear doors. <ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>




  • 8cm speakers are installed on the back door and instrument panel. <ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>

SUBWOOFER <VEHICLES WITH ROCKFORD ACOUSTIC DESIGN PREMIUM SOUND SYSTEM>



A 25-cm dual voice coil subwoofer is installed on the lower quarter trim (L.H.) in the luggage compartment, thus enhancing low-tone sound.
