Inspection Procedure 1: Communication between the M.U.T.-III and the SRS-ECU is not possible.


M15204000261ENG0000010000





COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the M.U.T.-III can not communicate with the SRS system, the CAN bus lines may be defective. If the SRS system does not work, the SRS-ECU or its power supply circuit may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged wiring harness or connector
  • Malfunction of the SRS-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics


Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Check and repair the power supply circuit system (Refer to ).
