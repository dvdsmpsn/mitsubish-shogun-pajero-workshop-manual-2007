DOOR INSIDE HANDLE KNOB PLAY CHECK



1. Check that the door inside handle knob play (the stroke until the door latch unlocks) meets the standard value.
Standard value (B): 10.5 mm
2. Check the door inside handle and door latch assembly, and replace them if they are out of the standard value.

DOOR INSIDE HANDLE LOCK KNOB STROKE CHECK



1. Remove the door trim assembly (Refer to GROUP 52A, Door Trim ).
2. Check that the stroke of the door inside handle lock knob meets the standard value.
Standard value (B): 15 mm

3. If it is outside the standard value, adjust the stroke of the inside handle lock knob by using the outer cable end, which connects the inside handle lock knob to the inside lock cable.
