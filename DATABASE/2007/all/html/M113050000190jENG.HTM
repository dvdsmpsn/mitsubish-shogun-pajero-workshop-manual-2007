REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> FUEL HIGH-PRESSURE TUBE DISCONNECTION



1. Push up the double lock of the fuel high-pressure tube connector.

2. Disconnect the fuel high-pressure tube while pushing the lock of the fuel high-pressure tube connector.

<<B>> FUEL TANK BAND/FUEL FILLER NECK BREATHER HOSE/FUEL TANK HARNESS CONNECTOR/FUEL TANK ASSEMBLY REMOVAL


1.
cautionWhen moving the stabilizer bar to the position where it will not interfere with the fuel tank assembly removal, be careful not to damage the ball joint section of the stabilizer link.
Before removing the fuel tank assembly, remove the clamp of the stabilizer bar, and move the stabilizer bar to the position where it will not interfere with the fuel tank assembly removal. (Refer to GROUP 34 - Stabilizer Bar ).

2. Support the fuel tank assembly using the transmission jack, and remove the fuel tank band and fuel tank assembly mounting nuts.
3. Using the transmission jack while taking care of the fuel filler neck breather hose and the fuel tank harness, lower the fuel tank assembly until the fuel filler neck breather hose and the fuel tank harness installation position over the fuel tank assembly can be checked.
4. Disconnect the fuel filler neck breather hose and the fuel tank harness connector. Then, remove the fuel tank assembly.
5. After removal of the fuel tank assembly, return the stabilizer bar to the original position, and install the clamp temporarily.

<<C>> FUEL TANK GAUGE UNIT REMOVAL


cautionPay attention not to damage float of the fuel tank gauge unit when removing the fuel tank gauge unit from the fuel tank.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< FUEL TANK GAUGE UNIT INSTALLATION


cautionPay attention not to damage and the float of the fuel tank gauge unit when installing it to the fuel tank.

>>B<< FUEL HIGH-PRESSURE TUBE INSTALLATION



cautionAfter connecting the fuel high-pressure tube, slightly pull it to ensure that it is installed securely. Also confirm that there is a play approximately 3 mm.
Connect the fuel high-pressure tube to the fuel tank module assembly.

>>C<< FUEL TANK ASSEMBLY/FUEL TANK HARNESS CONNECTOR/FUEL FILLER NECK BREATHER HOSE/FUEL TANK BAND INSTALLATION


1.
cautionWhen moving the stabilizer bar to the position where it will not interfere with the fuel tank assembly installation, be careful not to damage the ball joint section of the stabilizer link.
Before installing the fuel tank assembly, remove the clamp of the stabilizer bar, which was installed temporarily, and move the stabilizer bar to the position where it will not interfere with the fuel tank assembly installation.

2. Place the fuel tank assembly on the transmission jack.
3. Raise the fuel tank to the position where the fuel filler neck breather hose and the fuel tank harness connector can be connect.
4. Connect the fuel filler neck breather hose and the fuel tank harness connector.
5. Use the transmission jack to install the fuel tank assembly to the vehicle.
6. Install the fuel tank band, and then tighten the fuel tank band mounting bolts and the fuel tank assembly mounting nuts to the specified torque and fix the fuel tank assembly.
Tightening torque: 26 ± 4 N·m
7. After removal of the fuel tank assembly, return the stabilizer bar to the original position, and install the clamp. (Refer to GROUP 34 - Stabilizer Bar ).

>>D<< FUEL HIGH-PRESSURE TUBE CONNECTION



1. Before installing fuel high-pressure tube, push up the double lock of the fuel high-pressure tube connector.

2.
cautionAfter connecting the fuel high-pressure tube, slightly pull it to ensure that it is installed securely. Also confirm that there is a play approximately 3 mm.
Install the fuel high-pressure tube connector to the fuel pipe securely, and push down the double lock of the connector to lock it thoroughly.
