The following changes have been made to the controls of the 4G69-MIVEC-MPI engine provided on the GRANDIS.
Improvement / Additions
Remark
Addition of variable induction system
Improvement of torque at low or middle engine speed and improvement of output at high engine speed
Addition of engine oil pressure switch (for MIVEC)
Detect functioning of MIVEC
Addition of manifold absolute pressure sensor due to use of fuel returnless system
Correction of fuel injection volume based on manifold absolute pressure detected by manifold absolute pressure sensor
