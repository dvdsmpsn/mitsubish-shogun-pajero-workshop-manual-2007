REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>>CRANKSHAFT PULLEY BOLT REMOVAL



1. Use the special tool Flywheel stopper (MD998781), hold the flywheel.
2. Remove the crankshaft pulley bolt.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<<CRANKSHAFT BOLT / SPECIAL WASHER / CRANKSHAFT PULLEY INSTALLATION



1. Use the special tool Flywheel stopper (MD998781), hold the flywheel.

2. Clean and then degrease the crankshaft sprocket contacting surface of the crankshaft pulley.
noteDegreasing is necessary to prevent decrease in the friction between contacting surfaces.
3. Clean the bolt hole in the crankshaft, the crankshaft contacting surface and washer contacting surface of the crankshaft pulley, and the washer.
4. Apply an appropriately small amount of oil to the threads and seating surface of the crankshaft bolt.
5. Tighten the crankshaft bolt to the specified torque of 50 N·m.
6. Tighten the cylinder head bolts 90 degrees in the specified order.
7. Loosen all bolts fully in the reverse order of tightening.

8. Tighten the crankshaft bolt to the specified torque of 50 N·m.
9. Tighten the cylinder head bolts 90 degrees in the specified order.
