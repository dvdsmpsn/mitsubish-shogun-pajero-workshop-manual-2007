REMOVAL SERVICE POINT


<<A>> ROCKER ARMS AND ROCKER ARM SHAFT REMOVAL


When the rocker arm is removed from the rocker shaft, tag them to show the installation location for the reinstallation of the rocker arm, the T-lever, the piston arm assembly and so on.

<<B>> ROCKER ARMS AND SHAFT REMOVAL



cautionIf the lash adjuster is re-used, clean the lash adjuster. (Refer to lash adjuster inspection .)
Set special tool Lash adjuster holder (MD998443) to prevent the lash adjuster from coming free and falling to the floor.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< CAMSHAFT INSTALLATION



cautionUse care to prevent confusion of the right and left bank camshafts.
1. Apply engine oil to the camshaft journals and cams and then install the camshafts.
noteThe camshaft with a longer overall length is for the left bank.

2. Check to see that the dowel pin of the camshaft is located at the position shown.

>>B<< LASH ADJUSTER INSTALLATION


cautionIf the lash adjuster is re-used, clean the lash adjuster. (Refer to lash adjuster inspection .)

Fit the lash adjuster onto the rocker arm without allowing diesel fuel to spill out. Fit special tool Lash adjuster holder (MD998443) to prevent the lash adjuster from coming free and falling to the floor.

>>C<< ADJUSTING SCREW INSTALLATION



When installing the screw to the rocker arm temporarily, project it slightly 3mm from the same face level of the rocker arm end as shown.

>>D<< ROCKER ARMS AND SHAFT INSTALLATION



1. Position the end with the larger chamfer at the right on the front bank, and at the left on the rear bank.
noteThe side with the four bolt holes is on the intake side.
2. Position the side with the oil holes on the lower side (cylinder head side).

3. Check that notches in the each rocker shaft are facing the direction shown in the illustration.
4. Install the rocker shaft cap as its identification mark is in the direction shown.
5. Tighten the inlet side bolts to the specified torque of 31 ± 3 N·m.
6. Tighten the exhaust side bolts to the specified torque of 13 ± 1 N·m.
7. Remove special tool Lash adjuster holder (MD998443).

>>E<< CAMSHAFT OIL SEAL INSTALLATION




1. Apply engine oil to the lip area of the oil seal and the front end outside diameter of camshaft.
2. Use special tools Camshaft oil seal installer (MD998713) and Camshaft oil seal installer adapter (MB991559) to install the camshaft oil seal.
