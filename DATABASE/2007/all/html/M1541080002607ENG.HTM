Inspection Procedure 16: Distortion on FM Only.


M15410800026ENG0000010000


DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check with another broadcasting station.

Q. Is there distortion when turning to another broadcasting station?

Go to Step 2.
The signal from that station is too weak.
STEP 2. Relocate the reception area and check.

Q. When relocating the reception area does the distortion increase or decrease?

The cause may be multipath noise.
Repair or replace the radio and CD player. Check that a distortion is resolved.
