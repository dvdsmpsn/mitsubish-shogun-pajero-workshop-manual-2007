A11. REPLACE AIR CLEANER ELEMENT


M60200000015ENG0000010000


The air cleaner element will become dirty and loaded with dust during use, and the filtering effect will be substantially reduced. Replace it with a new one.

1. Unclasp the air cleaner cover clip.
2. Remove the air cleaner element and install a new one.
3. Be sure to close the air cleaner cover completely when clamping it.
