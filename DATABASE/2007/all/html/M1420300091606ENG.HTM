INSPECTION PROCEDURE 1: Central Door Locking System does not Work. <Not using SWS monitor>


M14203000916ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENT ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the central door locking system does not work at all, the ETACS-ECU may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the central door lock operation with door lock switch.


Use the door lock switch to check that the central door locking system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Refer to .

STEP 2. Check the power supply circuit.


When the ignition switch is turned to the LOCK (OFF) position, check if the hazard warning lamps illuminate.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Refer to GROUP 54Ad, inspection procedure A-1 "Check the battery power supply circuit to the ETACS-ECU <Not using SWS monitor> or <Using SWS monitor>."

STEP 3. Connector check: C-419 ETACS-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 4. Check the wiring harness between C-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.2 and fusible link (2).








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-240 and junction block connector D-404, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the power supply line for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 5. Retest the system.


Check that the central door locking system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the ETACS-ECU.
