Removal steps <Side turn-signal lamp integrated in door mirror type>

Mirror (Refer to GROUP 51, Outside mirror ).

Side turn-signal lamp assembly


4.
Approach lamp bulb

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT <FENDER PANEL ATTACHMENT TYPE SIDE TURN-SIGNAL LAMP>



Push the side turn-signal lamp toward the vehicle rear to bend the hook, and then remove by disengaging the tab from the fender panel.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT <FENDER PANEL ATTACHMENT TYPE SIDE TURN-SIGNAL LAMP>



Engage the claw to the fender panel to install the side turn signal lamp.
