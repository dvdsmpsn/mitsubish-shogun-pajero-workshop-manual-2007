INSPECTION PROCEDURE 12: Wiper deicer does not operate


M15102100007ENG0000010001




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the rear window defogger does not work after switching on, the rear window defogger relay circuit (wiper deicer power supply circuit) or its earth circuit may be defective. The wiper deicer or its relay may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the wiper deicer
  • Malfunction of the wiper deicer relay
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
