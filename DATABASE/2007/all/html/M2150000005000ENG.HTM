INLET MANIFOLD <6G75>


M21500000050ENG0000010001




The air intake port of the each cylinder on the intake manifold has been divided into the primary port. and the secondary port. The control valve has been installed to the secondary port, and this control valve is opened or closed through the vacuum actuator depending on the engine speed. That is an air intake control system which has been adopted. This system has prevented the typical 4-valve engine low performance at the low speed and improved the fuel economy within the range of the low to the middle speed. That is, this system uses the primary port only at the low speed and then makes the intake airflow speed high, so increases the inertia supercharging effect.

INLET MANIFOLD <4M41>



This 4M41 is designed to be a cross flow to improve an intake efficiency.
An inlet manifold corresponding to the cross flow is used.

SWIRL CONTROL VALVE <4M41>




The swirl control valve closes one of the intake ports during intake stroke. This creates a strong swirl inside the cylinder. The swirl in turn generates vortices in the air-fuel mixture introduced through the intake port.
The swirl moment remains working even during the subsequent compression stroke and increases the propagation speed of flame, thus helping combustion to be more stabilized
