Inspection Procedure 2: MPI system-related diagnosis code No.P0513 is set, and communication between M.U.T.-III and immobilizer cannot be established. <3,800>


M15410700017ENG0000010001




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The immobilizer-ECU communicates with the M.U.T.-III via the engine-A/T-ECU. The communication error between the immobilizer ECU and the engine-A/T-ECU may be occurred.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of immobilizer ECU power supply and earth
  • Damaged harness wires between the engine-A/T-ECU and the immobilizer ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-310 key reminder switch connector and D-216 engine-A/T-ECU connector






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 2. Check the harness wires between D-310 key reminder switch connector terminal No.7 and D-216 engine-A/T-ECU connector terminal No.65.








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-229, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 3. Check the immobilizer-ECU power supply circuit and earth circuit.


Refer to Inspection Procedure 4 "Check the immobilizer ECU power supply circuit and earth circuit" .

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the power supply and earth circuit to the immobilizer-ECU.

STEP 4. Retest the system.


Check that immobilizer ECU communicates with the M.U.T.-III.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Intermittent Malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
Replace the immobilizer-ECU and register the ignition key. (Refer to .) Then go to Step 5.

STEP 5. Retest the system.


Check that immobilizer ECU communicates with the M.U.T.-III.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Replace the engine-A/T-ECU and register the ignition key. (Refer to .)
