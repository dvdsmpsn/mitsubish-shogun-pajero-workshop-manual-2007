SIDE AND CURTAIN AIR BAGS


M25201000008ENG0000010000




SRS-ECU uses data of the side impact sensor (front [centre pillar area]), side impact sensor (rear [rear quarter panel area]), and G-sensor (in SRS-ECU) to calculate collision severity, during side collision. SRS-ECU judges necessity of side-air bag and curtain air bag based on the calculated collision severity.
