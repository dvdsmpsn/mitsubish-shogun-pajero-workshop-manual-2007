Inspection Procedure 2: Check the Centre Display Power Supply Circuit.


M15410800390ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The earth circuit, the ignition switch (ACC) circuit or the ignition switch (IG1) circuit is suspected to be open or defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the multi centre display
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the following connector: D-106 multi centre display connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 2. Measure the voltage at D-106 multi centre display connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the Ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position.

(3)Voltage between D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.10 and body earth
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Check the harness wire between battery (fusible link No.1) and D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.10.






notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-241, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the battery power supply open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 11.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 4. Measure the voltage at D-106 multi centre display connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the "ACC" position.

(3)Voltage between D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.13 and body earth
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Go to Step 5.

STEP 5. Check the harness wire between ignition switch (ACC) and D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.13.






notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-238 and D-241, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the ACC power supply open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Retest the system.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Measure the voltage at D-106 multi centre display connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.

(3)Voltage between D-106 centre display or multi display station connector terminal No.10 and body earth
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Go to Step 7.

STEP 7. Check the harness wire between ignition switch (IG1) and D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.36.






notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-241, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the IG1 power supply open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 11.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 8. Measure the resistance at D-106 multi centre display connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.

(2)Resistance between D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.1 and body earth
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less)

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10.
Go to Step 9.

STEP 9. Check the harness wire between D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.1 and body earth.





  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 11.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 10. Replace the multi centre display temporarily and retest the system.


Check that the multi centre display correctly when the multi centre display is replaced temporarily.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the multi centre display.
Go to Step 11.

STEP 11. Retest the system.


Check that the multi centre display correctly.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Return to Step 1.
