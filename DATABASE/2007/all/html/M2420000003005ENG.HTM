IMPACT SAFETY BODY RISE (REINFORCED IMPACT SAFETY EVOLUTION)






The following construction has been adopted for the impact safety body RISE. This structure alleviates the shocks which may be experienced by passengers in the event of a front, rear or side impact and maintains enough space inside the passenger compartment to preserve life and to improve passenger rescue.

  1. The high energy-absorbing structure includes front sidemembers with a straight frame and a large cross-section.
  2. The high energy-absorbing structure includes clashable zones at the front and rear of the front sidemembers to disperse the force of an impact.
  3. The joints between the front pillars and front deck crossmember maintain the high rigidity of the cabin.
  4. Large reinforcements have been added to each pillar to increase the rigidity of the joints along th e whole of the side of the vehicle body.
  5. The high energy-absorbing structure includes rear floor sidemembers with a straight frame and a large cross-section.
