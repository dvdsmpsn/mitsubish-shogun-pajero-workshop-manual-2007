Inspection Procedure 9: The PTC heater (Passenger’s side) does not work <3200>


M15504000178ENG0000010001




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


When the PTC heater does not work under the following conditions, the PTC heater circuit system may be defective.
PTC heater operating conditions
  • Air outlet: FOOT/DEF, FOOT, FACE/FOOT
  • Temperature control dial: MAX HOT
  • Coolant temperature: 82 degrees Celsius or less
noteWhen the coolant temperature becomes above 82 degrees Celsius and PTC heater stops the operation, the PTC heater does not operate until coolant temperature becomes below 72 degrees Celsius.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors
  • Malfunction of the PTC heater relay 2
  • Malfunction of the PTC heater 2
  • Malfunction of the automatic A/C control panel (A/C-ECU)
