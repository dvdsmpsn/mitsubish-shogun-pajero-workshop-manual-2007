PTC HEATER RELAY CONTROL


M21303000301ENG0000010000





When the following conditions are met on a cold engine, the engine-ECU turns ON the PTC heater relay to actuate the PTC heater.
PTC heater relay ON conditions:
  • 12 seconds have elapsed after the engine started.
  • Engine coolant temperature: 80 °C or less
  • Heater temperature setting: Maximum
  • Blower fan: ON
  • Glow plug relay: OFF or 2 seconds later from ON
