REMOVAL SERVICE POINT


<<A>> A/T DRIVE PLATE BOLT REMOVAL



1. Use special tool flywheel stopper (MD998781) to secure the A/T drive plate.
2. Remove the A/T drive plate bolts.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< CRANKSHAFT REAR OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



1. Apply a small amount of engine oil to the entire inner diameter of the crankshaft rear oil seal lip.
2. Use special tool crankshaft rear oil seal installer (MD998718) to press-fit the crankshaft rear oil seal.

>>B<< CRANKSHAFT A/T DRIVE PLATE ADAPTER/A/T DRIVE PLATE/A/T DRIVE PLATE ADAPTER PLATE INSTALLATION



Install the crankshaft A/T drive plate adapter and A/T drive plate adapter plate on the A/T drive plate in the direction shown in the illustration.

>>C<< A/T DRIVE PLATE BOLT INSTALLATION



1. Use special tool flywheel stopper (MD998781) to secure the A/T drive plate in the same manner as removal.
2. Tighten the A/T drive plate bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 74 ± 2 N·m

>>D<< CRANKSHAFT FRONT OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



1. Apply a small amount of engine oil to the entire inner diameter of the crankshaft front oil seal lip.
2. Apply a small amount of engine oil to the outer diameter of special tool crankshaft front oil seal installer (guide) (MD998717) and install it to the crankshaft.
3. Use special tool crankshaft front oil seal installer (MD998717) to press-fit the crankshaft front oil seal.

>>E<< TIMING BELT TRAIN SPACER/CRANKSHAFT ANGLE SENSING BLADE/CRANKSHAFT CAMSHAFT DRIVE SPROCKET INSTALLATION



To prevent the crankshaft pulley center bolt from loosening, clean or degrease the crankshaft, the timing belt train spacer, the crankshaft angle sensing blade and the crankshaft camshaft drive sprocket at the shown positions.
