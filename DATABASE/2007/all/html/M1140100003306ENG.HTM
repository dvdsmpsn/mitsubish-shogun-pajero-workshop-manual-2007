INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT


>>A<< O-RING INSTALLATION


caution
  • Care must be taken not to permit engine oil or other greases to adhere to the O-ring.
  • When inserting the pipe, check to be sure that there is no sand, dirt, etc. on its inner surface.

Fit the O-ring to the groove in the water pump inlet pipe and cooling water outlet pipe. Then lubricate the O-ring and the inside of the water pump assembly, cylinder head water by-pass pipe and cooling water outlet fitting with water. Then insert the water pump inlet pipe and cooling water outlet pipe to the water pump assembly, cylinder head water by-pass pipe and cooling water outlet fitting with water.
