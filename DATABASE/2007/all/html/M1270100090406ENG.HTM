REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> KNUCKLE AND LOWER ARM DISCONNECTION



Position a wooden block to the lower arm as shown in the illustration and use the floor jack to remove the lower arm mounting bolt by compressing the coil spring.

<<B>> DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER ASSEMBLY REMOVAL


Support the differential carrier assembly lower part by jacking to remove the joint bolt and the differential carrier assembly.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT


>>A<< DIFFERENTIAL SIDE OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



Use special tool differential oil seal installer (MB991168) to press-fit the oil seal.

>>B<< KNUCKLE AND LOWER ARM CONNECTION



Position a wooden block to the lower arm as shown in the illustration and use the floor jack to install the lower arm mounting bolt by compressing the coil spring.

REAR SUSPENSION CROSSMEMBER BUSHING (FOR REAR DIFFERENTIAL MOUNT) REPLACEMENT


1. Remove the rear differential.

2. Use the following special tools to remove the bushing.

  • Base (B) (MB991050)
  • Bearing (MB991218)
  • Bolt (MB991162)
  • Bearing remover and installer base (MB991816)
noteWhen removing the bushing, insert special tool bolt (MB991162) from the forward of vehicle and fix the nut with a wrench.

3. Use special tools to press-fit the bushing.
noteWhen installing the bushing, insert special tool bolt (MB991162) from the forward of vehicle and fix the nut with a wrench. Press-fit the bushing until the bushing end face is flush with the rear suspension crossmember.
4. Install the rear differential.
