>>A<< FRONT AIR MIXING DAMPER CONTROL MOTOR AND POTENTIOMETER INSTALLATION




When installing the front air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer, ensure that the damper and the motor are in the DEF position.

>>B<< OUTSIDE/INSIDE AIR SELECTION DAMPER CONTROL MOTOR INSTALLATION



When installing the outside/inside air selection damper control motor, ensure that the damper and the motor are in the outside position.

>>C<< MODE SELECTION DAMPER CONTROL MOTOR AND POTENTIOMETER INSTALLATION




When installing the mode selection damper control motor and potentiometer, ensure that the damper and the motor are in the DEF position.
