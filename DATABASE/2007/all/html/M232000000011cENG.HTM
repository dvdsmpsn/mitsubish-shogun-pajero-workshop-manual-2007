For the engine and transmission mounts, the gravity-centre support engine type, which is the same as that for conventional PAJERO, has been adopted as follows: 4-point support type (two places at the engine and two places at the transmission) for M/T vehicles, and 3-point support type (two places at the engine and one place at the transmission) for A/T vehicles.

  • The engine mounts are liquid-filled mounts which improve ride comfort and reduce vibration and noise when idling.
  • The transmission mount provides elastic support (dual anti-vibration) at the transmission support and the transmission centermember support, to reduce the amount of vibration transmitted to the vehicle body.
  • Shear-type insulators have been adopted as the transmission mount insulators.
