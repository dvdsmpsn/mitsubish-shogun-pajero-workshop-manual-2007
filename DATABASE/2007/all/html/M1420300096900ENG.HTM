COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the keyless entry system does not work normally, the input signal circuits to the components below or the ETACS-ECU may be defective.

  • Key reminder switch
  • All of the door switches
  • Keyless entry transmitter
  • Driver's door lock actuator

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the key reminder switch
  • Malfunction of the door switches
  • Malfunction of the keyless entry transmitter
  • Malfunction of the driver's door lock actuator
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the operation of the central door locking system.


Check that the central door locking system works normally.

STEP 2. Check the power supply circuit.


When the ignition switch is turned to the LOCK (OFF) position, check if the hazard warning lamps illuminate.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Refer to GROUP 54Ad, inspection procedure A-1 "Check the battery power supply circuit to the ETACS-ECU <Not using SWS monitor> or <Using SWS monitor>."

STEP 3. Pulse check


Check the input signals below which are related to the keyless entry system.
System switch
Check condition
Key reminder switch
When the inserted ignition key is pulled out
Driver's door switch
Driver's door is opened while all the other doors are closed.
Passenger’s door switch
Passenger's door is opened while all the other doors are closed.
Rear door (RH) switch
Rear door (RH) is opened while all the other doors are closed
Rear door (LH) switch
Rear door (LH) door is opened while all the other doors are closed
Back door switch
Back door is opened while all the other doors are closed
Keyless entry transmitter switch
When the switch is turned from off to on
OK: The M.U.T.-III sounds or the voltmeter needle fluctuates.

Q. Are the check result normal?

YES (All the signals are received normally.) : Go to Step 4.
NO (The key reminder switch signal is not received.) : Refer to GROUP 54Ad, inspection procedure C-11 "The key reminder switch signal is not received ."
NO (All the door switch signals are not received.) : Refer to GROUP 54Ad, inspection procedure C-14 "All the door switch signals are not received <LH drive vehicles> or <RH drive vehicles>."
NO (The keyless entry transmitter switch signal is not received.) : Refer to GROUP 54Ad, inspection procedure C-20 "Each switch signal of the keyless entry transmitter is not received ."

STEP 4. Retest the system.


Check the keyless entry system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the ETACS-ECU.
