REAR DIFFERENTIAL LOCK DETECTION SWITCH CHECK


M12701000918ENG0000010000


1. Jack up the vehicle.
2. Remove the air pipe and air hose connection.

3. Connect a pressure gauge and air regulator to the air pipe.
4.
cautionDo not apply a higher pressure so as not to damage the air regulator.
Adjust the outside air pressure with the air regulator until the pressure gauge shows approx. 24.5 kPa.
5. Lock the wheel on one side of the vehicle, and slowly turn the wheel on the other side.
6. Check for continuity in the rear differential lock detection switch.
Measurement conditions
Specified condition
When air is applied
Continuity (less than 2 ohms)
When air is released
Open circuit
