Inspection Procedure D-4: The Luggage Compartment Lamp does not Illuminate Normally. <Vehicles without Keyless Entry System>


M15410900127ENG0000010002


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the luggage compartment lamp does not turn on and off normally, wiring harness connector(s) or the bulb may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the luggage compartment lamp bulbs
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

STEP 4. Connector check: E-107 luggage compartment lamp connector



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 5. Check the bulbs of the luggage compartment lamp.


Check that the luggage compartment lamp bulbs are not burned out.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Replace the luggage compartment lamp bulb.

STEP 6. Check the lugggage compartment lamp switch.


Check which position (ON or DOOR) of the luggage compartment lamp switch does not activate the relevant lamp.

Q. Which switch position does not activate the lamp?

YES (All of the lamps illuminate normally) : The procedure is complete.
NO (The lamp does not illuminates at neither the ON nor DOOR position) : Go to Step 7.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the DOOR position) : Go to Step 9.
NO (The lamp does not illuminate at the ON position) : Go to Step 11.

STEP 7. Connector check: D-419 ETACS-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 8. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.6 to E-107 luggage compartment lamp connector terminal No.3.











notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, E-128, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the luggage compartment lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 9. Connector check: D-419 ETACS-ECU connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10.

STEP 10. Check the wiring harness from D-419 ETACS-ECU connector terminal No.5 to E-107 luggage compartment lamp connector terminal No.1.











notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-130, E-128, joint connector D-34 and junction block connector D-410, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Replace the luggage compartment lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 11. Check the wiring harness from E-107 luggage compartment lamp connector terminal No.2 to body earth.





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector E-128 and repair if necessary.

  • Check the earth wires for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the luggage compartment lamp.
Repair the wiring harness.
