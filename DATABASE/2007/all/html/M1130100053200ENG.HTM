OIL FEEDER CONTROL VALVE CHECK


M11301000532ENG0000010000


OPERATION CHECK



1. Disconnect the oil feeder control valve connector.
2. Make sure that the oil feeder control valve generates a tick sound when the battery voltage is applied between the connector terminals at the oil feeder control valve side.
cautionTo prevent the coil from burning, keep the duration of the voltage application as short as possible.

Resistance between terminals


1. Disconnect the oil feeder control valve connector.
2. Measure the resistance between the terminals of the connector at the oil feeder control valve.
Standard value: 6.9 - 7.9 Ω (at 20°C)
3. If the resistance deviates from the standard value, replace oil feeder control valve.
