Inspection Procedure 18: Preset Station are Erased.


M15410800024ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The cause is probably a faulty radio CD player memory backup power supply circuit system.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged wiring harness or connector.
  • Malfunction of the radio and CD player or CD changer.

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: Radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209.




Q. Is radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209 in good condition?

Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace the damage component(s). Check that a memory is retained.

STEP 2. Voltage measurement at radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209.




(1)Disconnect radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209.

(2)Measure the voltage between terminal number 30 and earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Does the measured voltage correspond with this range?

Either repair or replace the radio and CD player or CD changer. Check that a memory is retained.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Check the harness wire between D-209 radio and CD player or CD changer connector terminal No.30 and fusible link (1).





notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-241, and repair if necessary.

Q. Are the wiring harness between D-209 radio and CD player or CD changer connector terminal No.30 and fusible link (1) in good condition?

Repair or replace the radio and CD player or CD changer. Check that a memory is retained.
Repair the harness wire. Check that a memory is retained.
