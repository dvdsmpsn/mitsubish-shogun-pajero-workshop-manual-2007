REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT REMOVAL



caution
  • Be careful not to damage joint boot by crimping with the bent propeller shaft joint.
  • Hang propeller shaft with wires to prevent it from falling.
1. Move the transfer shift lever to "2H".

2. Make mating marks on the flange yoke and the differential carrier companion flange. Remove the front propeller shaft.

<<B>> PIN REMOVAL



1. Remove one side of dust cover mounting part on the vacuum actuator assembly.
2. Match a pin with a pin hole and remove the pin with a punch.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< DIFFERENTIAL MOUNTING BRACKET ASSEMBLY



Tighten differential mounting insulator in the order of A, B, C, D or A, C, B, D as shown, in the left diagram.

>>B<< VACUUM HOSE INSTALLATION


Install the vacuum hoses so that they match the identification colours of the actuator assembly nipple.

>>C<< FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT INSTALLATION


cautionRemove grease from fastener threads before tightening. Fastener threads covered with oily products could become loose.
Install the front propeller shaft so that the mating marks of the flange yoke and the differential carrier companion flange are aligned.
