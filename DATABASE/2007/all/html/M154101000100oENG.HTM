Code No.B1201 Trouble of fuel information


M15410100010ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If only the fuel gauge does not operate, the fuel tank gauge unit, combination meter, wiring harness or connector(s) may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Malfunction of fuel tank gauge unit
  • Malfunction of combination meter

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: Fuel tank gauge unit connector E-15.




Q. Is fuel tank gauge unit connector E-15 in good condition?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Check the fuel gauge circuit.




(1)Disconnect fuel tank gauge unit connector E-15.

(2)Use special tool MB991219 to connect a test lamp (12 V - 3.4 W) between the wiring harness connector terminals 1 and 2.
(3)Turn the Ignition switch to "ON" position.
(4)Check if the test lamp illuminates.
OK: Illuminates

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

Go to Step 6.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Connector check: Combination meter connector D-02.




Q. Is combination meter connector D-02 in good condition?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the connector.

STEP 4. Check the wiring harness between combination meter connector D-02 (terminals 40 and 49) and fuel tank gauge unit connector E-15 (terminals 2 and 1).








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-221 and E-09, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the wiring harness between combination meter connector C-02 (terminal 31) and fuel gauge unit connector D-07 (terminal 1) in good condition?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the harness.

STEP 5. M.U.T.-III data list



  • Item No.7: Fuel gauge (Target)
OK: Fuel gauge and M.U.T.-III displayed values agree with each other.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 7.
Go to Step 6.

STEP 6. Retest the system.


Check that the fuel gauge works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Replace the fuel tank gauge unit and then go to Step 7.

STEP 7. Retest the system.


Check that the fuel gauge works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Replace the combination meter.
