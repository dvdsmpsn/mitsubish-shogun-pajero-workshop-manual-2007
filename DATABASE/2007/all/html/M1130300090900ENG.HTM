REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> FUEL SUPPLY PUMP PIPE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL


cautionBecause the fuel pressure in the fuel line is high, wait for 3 minutes or longer after the engine is stopped. Then, cover the fuel supply pump pipe with a rag or similar materials to prevent the fuel spill, and then disconnect the fuel supply pump pipe assembly.

<<B>> FUEL INJECTION NO.4 PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION NO.3 PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION NO.2 PIPE ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION NO.1 PIPE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL



Using special tool Fuel injection pipe wrench (MB992188), remove the fuel injection pipe assembly No.1 to No.4.

<<C>> COMMON RAIL ASSEMBLY REMOVAL



caution
  • When operating, do not support the common rail assembly by holding the rail pressure limiter and the pressure sensor at both ends.
  • Before the installation, make sure that there is no foreign object or rust to the thread and to the high-pressure pipe sheet surface.

INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< INJECTOR ASSEMBLY/ SUPPORT BOLT/INJECTOR SUPPORT/INJECTOR HOLDER BOLT/GASKET/FUEL LEAKAGE PIPE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION


1. Tighten the support bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 30.0 ± 1.5 N·m
2. Install the gasket, injector assembly and pivot boss

3. Apply the engine oil to the bearing surface and thread of injector holder bolts.
4. Temporarily fix the injector supporters.
5. Install the gaskets to the fuel leakage pipe assembly.
6. Tighten the eye bolts at the Injector assembly side to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 15 ± 2 N·m
7. Tighten the eye bolt at the cylinder head side to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 15 ± 2 N·m
8. Tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly mounting bolt to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 24 ± 3 N·m

9. Tighten the injector holder bolts according to the following procedure.
(1)Tighten the Injector holder bolts to 10 ± 1 N·m.
(2)
caution
  • If the bolt is tightened less than the specified lower limit of 80 degree angle, the bolt may become loose. Be sure to tighten correctly.
  • If the bolt is tightened in excess of the specified upper limit of 85 degree angle, loosen the bolt completely and repeat the entire procedures.
Using special tool angle gauge (MB991614), tighten the Injector supporter mounting bolts in the illustrated sequence by a further 80 to 85 degree angle.

>>B<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



1. Using a proper socket wrench, the oil seal is pressed and fit into the rocker cover.

>>C<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



Use special tools oil seal installer (MB992104) and handle (MB992075) to press-fit the oil seal.

caution
  • The reinstallation histories of the removed fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly are up to five times. To count how many times the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly is reinstalled, record the number of the reinstallation histories on the service booklet by adding this latest number of the histories, which is usually "1", to the previous one.Use a new fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly when the total reinstallation history numbers reach five times, or when the supply pump assembly or common rail assembly is replaced. In this case, record "a new fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly, the number of the reinstallation histories is zero" on the service booklet.
  • When the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pipe assembly is reinstalled, confirm there is no foreign material on the seal surface or in the pipe and then install it not to deviate from the axis, fitting the seal surface.
