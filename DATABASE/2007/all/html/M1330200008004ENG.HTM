STABILIZER LINK BALL JOINT DUST COVER REPLACEMENT


M13302000080ENG0000010001


Only when the dust cover is damaged accidentally during service work, replace the dust cover as follows:

1. Remove the clip ring, and then remove the dust cover.
2. Apply the multipurpose grease to the inside of the dust cover.
3. Wrap plastic tape around the stabilizer link stud, and then install the dust cover to the stabilizer link.
4. Secure the dust cover in place with the clip ring.
5. Press the dust cover with your finger to check that there are no cracks or damage in the dust cover.
