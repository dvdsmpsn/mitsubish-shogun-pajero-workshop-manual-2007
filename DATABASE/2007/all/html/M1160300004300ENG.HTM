caution
  • Never attempt to adjust the gap of the iridium plug.
  • Do not attempt to clean the spark plug because it may result in damage to the electrode. However, if remarkable carbon deposits must be removed, using a plug cleaner, complete the cleaning within 20 seconds to protect the electrode. Do not use a wire brush.
noteAs the prospective distance for this spark plug replacement is 100,000 km. If the plug gap and insulation resistance are normal, check the plug state and clean it if necessary.

SPARK PLUG INSULATION RESISTANCE CHECK



1. Measure the insulation resistance. If the insulation resistance of the spark plug is not within the limited value, clean the plug within 20 seconds using a plug cleaner.
2. After cleaning, measure the insulation resistance again. Replace the plug unless it is within the limited value.
Limit: 10 MΩ
