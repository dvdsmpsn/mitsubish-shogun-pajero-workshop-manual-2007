AUTOMATIC ANTI-GLARE MIRROR




Some models have an automatic anti-glare inside rear view mirror, which changes the reflectance of a mirror automatically depending on the outside brightness.

  • The automatic anti-glare inside rear view mirror incorporates a front light sensor, rear light detection sensor, and control unit. The front light detection sensor detects the outside brightness, and the rear light sensor detects the brightness of headlights coming from behind. Depending on the difference of the quantity of light, the quantity of reflection is controlled to reduce glare from behind.

  • When the vehicle is moving backward, the anti-glare function is deactivated to secure a clear rear view.

  • This system has an ON/OFF switch.
