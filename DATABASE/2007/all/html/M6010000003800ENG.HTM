29. BRAKES


M60100000038ENG0000010000


1. Service Brake
Put the vehicle in gear and apply the brakes while the vehicle is in motion. Be sure brake operation is smooth and positive.
2. Parking Brake
(1)Stop the vehicle on a steep incline.
With the service brakes firmly applied, place the transmission in "N" position, and set the parking brakes.
(2)Slowly release the service brakes to see if the parking brakes will hold.
