NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



1. Cut the side outer panel as shown in the illustration to separate the weld points of the front pillar reinforcement and side sill reinforcement at the side sill section.

2. After removing the front pillar, in order to remove the front pillar reinforcement remaining on the body side, cut the side outer panel 120 mm above the cut area.

3. After removing the front pillar reinforcement, remove the front pillar inner upper remaining on the body side.

INSTALLATION



1. Align the front pillar inner upper and front pillar reinforcement, and open a hole from the location shown in the front pillar reinforcement for filling foam materials between the front pillar reinforcement and side outer panel.

2. When assembling the new front pillar inner lower parts, apply in advance body sealant to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.

3. When assembling the new front pillar inner upper part, apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

4. When installing the new front pillar reinforcement, apply the body sealant in advance to the areas shown in the figure. Also, to prevent the foam material from dripping, adhere the urethane foam so that the front pillar inside is filled.

5. When installing the new front pillar outer, apply the body sealant and adhesives in advance to the areas shown in the figure. Also, to prevent the foam material from dripping, adhere the urethane foam so that the front pillar inside is filled.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

6. Assemble the front outer pillar, then cover the hole and flange with bolt and aluminium tape and fill the hole with foam materials as shown in the figure of the instructions.
Foam:
3M ATD ULTRAPRO Panel foam-Yellow

7. Wait 2 hours after filling the foam materials to remove the bolt and aluminium tape. Then melt the foam materials with a soldering gun so a clip, etc. can thoroughly be inserted in the hole that was clogged with foam materials, to bore open the hole.
