Code No. P0325: Detonation Sensor System


M11310100224ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • The sensor signal is input to the engine-A/T-ECU (terminal No. 93) from the detonation sensor (terminal No. 2).
  • The power voltage is earthed to the engine-A/T-ECU (terminal No. 103) from the detonation sensor (terminal No. 1).

FUNCTION



  • The detonation sensor detects the vibration of the cylinder block caused by detonation waves, and inputs a signal to the engine-A/T-ECU.
  • In response to the signal, the engine-A/T-ECU provides controls to retard the ignition timing when the detonation occurs.

TROUBLE JUDGMENT


Check Conditions
  • Engine speed is 2,500 r/min or more.
  • Volumetric efficiency is above 40 %.
Judgment Criterion
  • Change of detonation sensor output voltage (detonation sensor peak voltage in each 1/2 turn of the crankshaft) has not been 0.06 V or more in the last consecutive 200 periods.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed detonation sensor
  • Open/short circuit or harness damage in detonation sensor circuit or loose connector contact
  • Failed engine-A/T-ECU
