EMISSION REDUCTION SYSTEMS


M21703000011ENG0000010000


These decrease CO, HC and particulate matter in the exhaust gases and consist of fuel injection control and catalytic converter.

1.FUEL INJECTION CONTROL


Refer to GROUP13B <4M4> - Fuel injeciton control .

2.CATALYTIC CONVERTER



Catalytic converter is installed immediately after the turbocharger and in the center of the exhaust pipe under the floor. It oxidizes the CO and HC that are discharged by the engine.
Catalytic converter is a monolith with beehive design with catalysts on the unit surface. It is protected by a thermally insulating mat and enclosed in a shell.
