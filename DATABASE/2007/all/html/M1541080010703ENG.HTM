Inspection Procedure 10: Sound of a DVD (Video Disk) can be Played, but no Image is Played.


M15410800107ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The DVD (video disk) or the audio visual navigation unit may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Defective DVD (video disk)
  • Malfunctions ofaudio visual navigation unit

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Confirmation in MMCS service mode


Perform "Network/Connect Line Check" in the MMCS service mode, and check whether "OK" is displayed under "DVD drive". (Refer to .)

Q. Is "OK" displayed under "DVD drive" item?

Go to Step 2.
Replace the audio visual navigation unit.

STEP 2. Check a DVD (video disk) to be inserted.


Check that there are no stains or scratches on the DVD (video disk).

Q. Are there any stains or scratches on the DVD (video disk)?

Clean or deburr the disk, or use a scratch and burr-free disk. Then insert the disk again.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Substitute a known good DVD (video disk), and check the trouble symptom.


When a known good DVD (video disk) is loaded, check whether it is played normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the DVD (video disk).
Replace the audio visual navigation unit.
