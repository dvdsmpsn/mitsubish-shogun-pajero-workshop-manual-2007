Code No. P0551: Power Steering Fluid Pressure Switch System


M11310100335ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • The battery voltage is applied to the power steering fluid pressure switch (terminal No. 1) from the engine-A/T-ECU (terminal No. 47).

FUNCTION



  • It is detected whether a load is applied on the power steering fluid pump by steering or not, and the signal is inputted to the engine-A/T-ECU. When the power steering fluid pressure switch "ON" signal (a large load on the power steering fluid pump) is inputted, the engine-A/T-ECU provides the idle-up control.

TROUBLE JUDGMENT


Check Conditions
  • Engine coolant temperature is 30°C or higher.
  • Drive for 4 seconds or more with the vehicle speed is 50 km/h or more. Stop the vehicle (vehicle speed is 1.5 km/h or less). Repeat 10 time or more.
Judgment Criterion
  • Power steering pressure switch remains on.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed power steering fluid pressure switch
  • Open/short circuit in power steering fluid pressure switch circuit or loose connector contact
  • Failed engine-A/T-ECU
