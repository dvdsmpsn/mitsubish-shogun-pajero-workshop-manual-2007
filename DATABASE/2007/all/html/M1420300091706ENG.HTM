INSPECTION PROCEDURE 3: A Door can not be Locked or Unlocked by The Central Door Locking System. <LH drive vehicles>


M14203000917ENG0000010000




COMMENT ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If a door or the back door can not be locked or unlocked by the central door locking system, the door lock actuator or the back door lock actuator may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the door lock actuator
  • Malfunction of the back door lock actuator
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Confirm which door lock actuator is defective.


Q. Which door fails to lock correctly?

Driver's door : Go to Step 2.
