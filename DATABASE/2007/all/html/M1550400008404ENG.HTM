Inspection Procedure 12: Condenser fan does not operate. <3800>


M15504000084ENG0000010000




CIRCUIT OPERATION


If the A/C condenser fan does not operate when the A/C condenser fan relay system may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the A/C-ECU
  • Malfunction of the engine-A/T-ECU
  • Malfunction of the A/C condenser fan relay
  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors
  • Malfunction of the A/C condenser fan

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-212 engine-A/T-ECU connector and A-29 A/C condenser fan motor connector






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Voltage measurement at A-29 A/C condenser fan connector.




(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.

(3)Disconnect engine-A/T-ECU connector D-212 and earth terminal 17.

(4)Measure the voltage between terminal 1 and body earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 11.
Go to Step 3.

STEP 3. Connector check: A-10X A/C condenser fan relay connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the connector.

STEP 4. Check the A/C condenser fan relay continuity.


Refer to .

Q. Is the A/C condenser fan relay in good condition?

Go to Step 5.
Replace the A/C condenser fan relay.

STEP 5. Voltage measurement at A-10X A/C condenser fan relay connector.




(1)Remove the relay, and measure at the junction block side.

(2)Measure the voltage between terminal 5 and body earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 7.
Go to Step 6.

notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check joint connector A-16, and repair if necessary.

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).

(1)Remove the relay, and measure at the junction block side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position.

(3)Voltage between terminal 3 and body earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 9.
Go to Step 8.

STEP 8. Check the wiring harness between A-10X A/C condenser fan relay connector No.5 and ignition switch (IG2).








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check junction block connector D-403, D-401 and joint connector A-15, and repair if necessary.

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 9. Check the wiring harness between A-10X A/C condenser fan relay connector No.4 and D-212 engine-A/T-ECU connector No.17.








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector A-03 <LHD> or D-137 <RHD>, and repair if necessary.

Go to Step 10.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 10. Check the wiring harness between A-10X A/C condenser fan relay connector No.1 and A-29 A/C condenser fan connector No.1.








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check joint connector A-14, and repair if necessary.

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).

(1)Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
