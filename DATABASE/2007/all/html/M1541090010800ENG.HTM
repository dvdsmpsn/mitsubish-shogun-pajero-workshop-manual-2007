Inspection Procedure F-2: Neither Right nor Left Automatic Levelling Operates.


M15410900108ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the power supply circuit, the earth circuit and the communication circuit are normal.


OPERATION


When a vehicle is at a standstill or being driven at a constant speed for 12 seconds or more (except on rough road), the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU inputs a headlamp LO relay signal, vehicle speed signal, and height signal for judgement and operates the levelling.
noteThe automatic levelling during driving at a constant speed operates only once after starting driving. The automatic levelling does not work if the vehicle pitch angle during driving at a constant speed and the vehicle pitch angle when the vehicle is stopped are the same.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


Assuming that the automatic levelling diagnosis code is not set, if neither right nor left automatic levelling operates, the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU, wiring harness and connectors between the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU and the front-ECU (headlamp relay: LO), the vehicle speed sensor (vehicle signal abnormality), or wiring harness and connectors between the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU and the vehicle speed sensor may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU
  • Malfunction of the vehicle speed sensor
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors
