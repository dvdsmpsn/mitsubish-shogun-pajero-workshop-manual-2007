INITIALISATION OF SUNROOF


M14206000046ENG0000010000


If the sunroof motor assembly is removed or the anti-trap function is triggered at least consecutively 5 times, perform the initialisation below.
1. Using the open switch of sunroof switch, move the sunroof lid glass to the fully opened position.
2. Using the close/down switch of sunroof switch, move the sunroof lid glass from the fully opened position to the fully tilt-up position. During that time, when the close/down switch is pressed once, the sunroof lid glass is slid to the closing direction approximately 30 mm, and automatically stops.
3. When the sunroof lid glass is moved to the fully tilt-up position, press and hold the close/down switch of the sunroof switch for 0.5 seconds or more.
