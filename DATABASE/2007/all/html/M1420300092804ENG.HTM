INSPECTION PROCEDURE 4: Passenger's and/or Rear Power Window(s) do not work by means of The Power Window Main Switch. <LH drive vehicles>


M14203000928ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the passenger's and/or rear <Long wheelbase> power window does not work by means of the power window main switch, the power window main switch or the respective power window sub switch(es) may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the power window main switch
  • Malfunction of the front power window sub switch (RH), rear power window sub switch (RH) <Long wheelbase> or rear power window sub switch (LH) <Long wheelbase>
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the power window main switch.


Check that the driver's power window works by means of the power window main switch.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Refer to .

STEP 2. Check the power window sub switch.


Check that each power window works by means of the respective power window sub switch when the power window lock switch is turned off.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Refer to .

STEP 3. Determine a trouble spot.


Q. Which power window does not work when the power window main switch is operated?

Front passenger's door : Go to Step 4.
Rear right door <Long wheelbase> : Go to Step 7.
Rear left door <Long wheelbase> : Go to Step 10.

STEP 4. Connector check: F-02 power window main switch connector and F-11 front power window sub switch (RH) connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the connector.

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness between F-02 power window main switch connector terminal No.11 and F-11 front power window sub switch (RH) connector terminal No.6.






notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-114, D-133 and joint connector D-34, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the communication lines for open circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Retest the system.


After the front power window sub switch (RH) is replaced, check that the front passenger's door power window can be operated by the power window sub switch.
(1)Replace the front power window sub switch (RH).
(2)Check that the front passenger's power window works by means of the power window main switch.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00, How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the power window main switch.
