DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER OIL SEAL REPLACEMENT


M12601000906ENG0000010000


1. Remove the drive shaft LH (Refer to ).

2. Use special tool rear axle shaft oil seal remover (MB990590) to remove the oil seal .

3. Use the following special tools to install new oil seal.

  • Bearing and oil seal installer set (MB990934)
  • Bearing and oil seal installer set (MB990938)

4. Apply multipurpose grease to the oil seal to lip before installing the drive shaft LH (Refer to ).
caution
  • Be careful not damage the lip of oil seal.
  • Replace the circlip attached to the spline of LH DOJ side of driveshaft with a new one.
