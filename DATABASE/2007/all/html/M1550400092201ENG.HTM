DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINT


<<A>> ARMATURE REMOVAL


1. Remove the armature mounting bolt.
2. Tighten the M10 bolt in the armature bolt hole to disengage the shaft from the armature serration.

REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< A/C COMPRESSOR COIL INSTALLATION



Install the A/C compressor coil while aligning the pin hole of A/C compressor itself with the protrusion of A/C compressor coil

>>B<< SNAP RING INSTALLATION


Be careful not to expand the snap ring excessively. If the inside diameter of the snap ring exceeds 30.5 mm due to excessive expansion, replace it.

>>C<< AIR GAP ADJUSTMENT



Apply battery voltage to the magnetic clutch, and check that the clutch air gap is within the standard value. If not within the standard value, use a washer to adjust the air gap.
Standard value: 0.35 - 0.60 mm
