Inspection Procedure 1: Power of Radio and CD Player or CD Changer does not Turn ON when the Ignition Switch is in the "ACC" Position or "ON" Position.


M15410800009ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The cause is probably a faulty radio and CD player or CD changer power supply circuit system.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged wiring harness or connector.
  • Malfunction of radio and CD player or CD changer.

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check to see that the power turns ON when the power switch is turned ON.


(1)Turn the ignition switch to "ACC" position.
(2)Turn ON the radio and CD player or CD changer power switch.

Q. Is it possible to put the radio and CD player or CD changer power in the "ON" position?

Go to Step 2.
Go to Step 5.

STEP 2. Connector check: radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209.




Q. Is radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209 in good condition?

Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace the component(s). If the power switch is turned on, the radio and CD player or CD changer should operate normally.

STEP 3. Voltage measurement at radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209 in order to check the battery circuit of power supply system to the radio and CD player or CD changer (ignition switch ACC).




(1)Disconnect radio and CD player or CD changer connector D-209, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2)Turn the ignition switch to "ACC" position.

(3)Measure the voltage between terminal 21 and earth.
OK: System voltage

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Go to Step 4.

STEP 4. Check the harness wire between D-209 radio and CD player or CD changer connector terminal No.21 and ignition switch (ACC).






notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-209 and D-238, and repair if necessary.

Q. Are the wiring harnesses between D-209 radio and CD player or CD changer connector terminal No.21 and ignition switch (ACC) in good condition?

Go to Step 5.
Repair or replace the damage component(s). If the power switch is turned on, the radio and CD player or CD changer should operate normally.

STEP 5. Check the assembling state of the radio and CD player or CD changer.


noteThe radio and CD player or CD changer is earthed to the instrument panel centre reinforcement directly.

Q. Is the radio and CD player or CD changer installed correctly?

Repair or replace the radio and CD player or CD changer. If the power switch is turned on, the radio and CD player or CD changer should operate normally.
Install properly. If the power switch is turned on, the radio and CD player or CD changer should operate normally.
