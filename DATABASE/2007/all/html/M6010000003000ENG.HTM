23. WIPERS AND WASHERS


M60100000030ENG0000010000



1. Front wiper and washer
(1)Check operation of the front wipers in all ranges.
(2)Check the aim of the front washer stream.
(3)Check the wiper blade-stop positions.
(4)Verify that the interval between cycles of wiping is shifted when timer knob is turned to any position.
(5)Verify that the front wipers function by operating the washer switch.
2. Rear wiper and washer
(1)Check the operation of the rear wiper.
(2)Check the aim of the rear washer stream.
(3)Check the wiper blade-stop positions.
