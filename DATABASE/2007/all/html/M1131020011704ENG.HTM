Code No. P2010: Swirl Control Solenoid Valve Circuit High Input


M11310200117ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • Power is supplied to the swirl control solenoid valve (terminal No. 2) from the engine control relay (terminal No. 1).
  • The engine-ECU (terminal No. 10) makes the power transistor in the unit be in "ON" position, and that makes currents go on the swirl control solenoid valve (terminal No. 1).

FUNCTION



  • By the signals from the engine-ECU, the swirl control solenoid valve takes control switching the pressure in the vacuum tank between the vacuum pressure and the atmopheric pressure.

TROUBLE JUDGMENT


Check Conditions
  • Battery positive voltage is 8 - 16 V
  • 2 seconds later after the ignition switch has been in "ON" position or the engine has started up
  • Engine speed is 3,000 r/min or lower
Judgment Criterion
  • For three seconds, the swirl control solenoid valve position judged by the terminal voltage is in "Open Position".

PROBABLE CAUSE



  • Failed engine-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III diagnosis code



  • Reconfirmation of diagnosis code.
