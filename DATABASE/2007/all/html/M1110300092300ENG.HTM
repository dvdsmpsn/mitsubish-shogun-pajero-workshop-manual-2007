INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< INLET MANIFOLD INSTALLATION



1. Tighten the nuts on the right bank to 6.4 N·m.
2. Tighten the nuts on the left bank to the specified torque of 22 ± 1 N·m.
3. Tighten the nuts on the right bank to the specified torque.
4. Tighten the nuts on the left bank and those on the right bank again to 22 ± 1 N·m.

>>B<< INJECTOR INSTALLATION



cautionUse care not to let engine oil enter the delivery pipe.
1. Before installing the pressure regulator, lubricate the O-ring with a drop of new engine oil for easy installation.
2. Insert the injector top end into the delivery pipe. Be careful not to damage the O-ring during installation.
