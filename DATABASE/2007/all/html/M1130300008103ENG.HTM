INJECTOR CHECK


M11303000081ENG0000010000



1. Disconnect the injector connector.
2. Measure the resistance between terminals No. 1 and No. 2.
Standard value: Approximately 0.45 Ω (at 20°C)
3. If not within the standard value, replace the injector. After replacing the injector, register the injector identification code and learn fuel injection (Refer to GROUP 00 - Precautions Before Service - What The Common Rail Engine Learns ).
