Inspection Procedure 5: Obstacle is not Detected even when The Selector Lever is Shifted to "R" (Reverse) Position.


M15410300017ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If obstacle is not detected even when the selector lever is shifted to "R" (reverse) position, the wiring harness, connectors, inhibitor switch <A/T>, back-up lamp switch <M/T>, or corner sensor-ECU may be defective. In addition, obstacle is not detected when the ultrasonic noise is received.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Malfunction of the inhibitor switch <A/T> or back-up lamp switch <M/T>
  • Malfunction of corner sensor-ECU
  • Malfunction of reversing sensor (corner sensor/back sensor)

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the reversing sensor (corner sensor/back sensor) for interference.


Q. Are there anything that interferes with the communication (things that generate radio waves such as magnets and an air-cleaning device that has a power plug) near the reversing sensor (corner sensor/back sensor)?

Move away or remove things that interfere with the communication (things that generate radio waves such as magnets and a air-cleaning device that has a power plug) near the reversing sensor (corner sensor/back sensor).
Go to Step 2.

STEP 2. Connector check: D-16 corner sensor-ECU connector and C-05 inhibitor switch connector <A/T> or C-17 back-up lamp switch connector <M/T>.






Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 3. Check the wiring harness between C-05 inhibitor switch connector (terminal No. 8) <A/T> or C-17 back-up lamp switch connector (terminal No. 1) <M/T> and D-16 corner sensor-ECU connector (terminal No. 1).








noteBefore the wiring harness inspection, inspect the intermediate connectors D-225, and D-230, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 4.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 4. Connector check: G-08 back sensor (RH) connector.



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 5. Check the wiring harness between G-08 back sensor (RH) connector (terminal No. 1, 2) and D-16 corner sensor-ECU connector (terminal No. 9, 10).







noteBefore the wiring harness inspection, inspect the intermediate connectors E-108 and D-222, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 6. Connector check: G-11 back sensor (LH) connector.



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 7.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 7. Check the wiring harness between back sensor (LH) connector G-11 (terminal No. 1, 2) and D-16 corner sensor-ECU connector (terminal 6, 10).







noteBefore the wiring harness inspection, inspect the intermediate connectors E-108 and D-222, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 8.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 8. Connector check: Corner sensor (rear: RH) connector E-110-1.




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 9.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 9. Check the wiring harness between E-110-1 corner sensor (rear: RH) connector (terminal No. 1, 2) and D-16 corner sensor-ECU connector (terminal 8, 10).








noteBefore the wiring harness inspection, inspect the intermediate connectors E-110, E-119, and D-222, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 10. Connector check: E-113-1 corner sensor (rear: LH) connector.




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 11.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 11. Check the wiring harness between E-113-1 corner sensor (rear: LH) connector (terminal No. 1, 2) and D-16 corner sensor-ECU connector (terminal 7, 10).








noteBefore the wiring harness inspection, inspect the intermediate connectors E-113, E-119, and D-222, and repair if necessary.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 12.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 12. Verification after back sensor (RH) replacement


After replacing the back sensor (RH), verify that the reversing sensor system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Go to Step 13.

STEP 13. Verification after back sensor (LH) replacement


After replacing the back sensor (LH), verify that the reversing sensor system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Go to Step 14.

STEP 14. Verification after corner sensor (rear: RH) replacement


After replacing the corner sensor (RH), verify that the reversing sensor system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Go to Step 15.

STEP 15. Verification after corner sensor (rear: LH) replacement


After replacing the corner sensor (rear: LH), verify that the reversing sensor system works normally.

Q. Is the check result normal?

The procedure is complete.
Replace the corner sensor-ECU.
