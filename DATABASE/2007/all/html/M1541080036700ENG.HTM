Inspection Procedure 8: No Sound is Output from the Wireless Headphone.


M15410800367ENG0000010000


DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Speaker check

When the speaker sound is set to the rear monitor output, check whether the rear monitor sound is output from the speakers.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Refer to Inspection Procedure 6 "The rear monitor sound is not output from the speaker" .
STEP 2. Wireless headphone battery check

Replace the wireless headphone battery, and check the wireless headphone sound.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the connector concerned.
STEP 3. Replace the wireless headphone temporarily, and check the trouble symptom.

Replace the wireless headphone temporarily, and check if sound is output from the wireless headphone.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the wireless headphone.
Replace the rear monitor.
