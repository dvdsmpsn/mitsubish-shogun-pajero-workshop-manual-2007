Inspection Procedure 2: Left or right side electric retractable remote controlled door mirror does not work.


M15102200007ENG0000010001


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If left or right side electric retractable remote controlled door mirror does not work at all, electric retractable remote controlled door mirror may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the door mirror assembly
  • Malfunction of the retractor motor
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
