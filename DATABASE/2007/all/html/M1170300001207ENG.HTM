POSITIVE CRANKCASE VENTILATION VALVE CHECK


M11703000012ENG0000010000



1. Insert a thin rod into the positive crankcase ventilation valve from the side shown in the illustration (rocker cover installation side), and move the rod back and forth to check that the plunger moves.
2. If the plunger does not move, there is a clogging in the positive crankcase ventilation valve. In this case, clean or replace the positive crankcase ventilation valve.
