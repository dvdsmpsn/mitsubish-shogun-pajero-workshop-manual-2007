The inspection procedure below


Inspection Procedure 3: When the Ignition Switch is Turned to "ON" Position (Engine Stopped), the SRS Warning Lamp does not Illuminate.
Inspection Procedure 4: After the Ignition Switch is Turned to the "ON" Position, the SRS Warning Lamp does not Go Off within Approximately Seven Seconds.


M15204000104ENG0000010000




TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION (COMMENT)



  • The SRS-ECU sends the SRS warning lamp signal to the combination meter via the CAN communication.
  • This may be caused by faults in the CAN bus line, the combination meter or the SRS-ECU.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Damaged wiring harness or connector
  • Combination meter defect
  • Malfunction of the SRS-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics


Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Replace the SRS-ECU (Refer to ).
Repair the CAN bus lines (Refer to GROUP 54D, Diagnosis ).
