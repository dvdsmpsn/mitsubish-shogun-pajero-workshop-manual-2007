>>A<< GLOW PLUG INSTALLATION


1.
cautionCheck that the glow plug, glow plug mounting thread and plug seating surface of cylinder head are dry.
Install the glow plug to the cylinder head.

2. Tighten the glow plug as follows.
(1)Tighten the glow plug to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 9.0 ± 1.0 N·m
(2)
caution
  • If the glow plug is tightened less than the specified lower limit of 30 degree angle, the glow plug may become loose. Be sure to tighten correctly.
  • If the glow plug is tightened in excess of the specified upper limit of 40 degree angle, loosen the glow plug completely and repeat the entire procedures.
Using special tool angle gauge (MB991614), tighten the glow plug by a further 30 to 40 degree angle.
