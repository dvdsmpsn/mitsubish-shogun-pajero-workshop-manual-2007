HEADLAMP WASHER MOTOR CHECK



1. With the headlamp washer motor installed to the washer tank, fill the washer tank with washer fluid.
2. Check that the washer fluid squirts out strongly when battery voltage is applied to terminal (1) and terminal (2) is earthed.

HEADLAMP WASHER SWITCH CHECK


noteThe headlamp washer switch is integrated in the column-ECU, and can not be checked. However, its operation can be checked by the input signal check (Refer to GROUP 54Ad - ETACS, Input signal chart ).
