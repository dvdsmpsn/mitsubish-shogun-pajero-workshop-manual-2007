INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS


>>A<< INJECTOR HOLDER SUPPORTCRANKSHAFT BOLT / INJECTOR HOLDER BOLT / SUPPORT BOLT / FUEL LEAKAGE PIPE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION



1. Apply engine oil to the threaded portion and seating face of the support bolt.
2. Tighten the support bolt to specified torque 30 ± 1.5 N·m.
3. Apply engine oil to the threaded portion and seating face of the injector holder bolt.
4. Temporarily tighten the injector holder bolt.
5. Temporarily tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly at injector. Temporarily tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly at cylinder head and cam cap.
6. Tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly bolt to specified torque 15 ± 2 N·m at injector side.
7. Tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly bolt to specified torque 15 ± 2 N·m at cylinder head side.
8. Tighten the fuel leakage pipe assembly bolt to specified torque 24 ± 4 N·m at cam cap side.
9. Tighten the injector holder bolt to specified torque 10 ± 1 N·m.

10.
caution
  • If the bolt is tightened less than the specified lower limit of 80 degree angle, the bolt may become loose. Be sure to tighten correctly.
  • If the bolt is tightened in excess of the specified upper limit of 85 degree angle, loosen the nut completely and repeat the entire procedures.
Using the special tool Angle gauge (MB991614), tighten the bearing cap bolts in the illustrated sequence by a further 80 to 85 degree angle.

>>B<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



1. Using a proper socket wrench, the oil seal is pressed and fit into the rocker cover.

>>C<< OIL SEAL INSTALLATION



1. Using the special tool Oil seal installer (MB992104) and handle (MB992075), the oil seal is pressed and fit into the rocker cover.
