Inspection Procedure 26: Oil pressure switch system


M11310100331ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • Battery voltage is applied to the oil pressure switch (terminal No. 1) from the engine-A/T-ECU (terminal No. 27).

FUNCTION



  • Detect the engine oil pressure conditions and enter the result into the engine-A/T-ECU. After starting the engine if the oil pressure rises to higher than the given pressure, the engine oil pressure switch turns OFF.Under this condition the engine-A/T-ECU sends the command of turning off the oil warning lamp to the combination meter via CAN.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed oil pressure switch
  • Open/short circuit or damage in the oil pressure switch circuit or loose connector contact
  • Failed engine-A/T-ECU
