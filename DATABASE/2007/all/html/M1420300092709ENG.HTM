INSPECTION PROCEDURE 3: Relevant Power Window(s) do not Work by means of the Front Passenger's Power Window Sub Switch and Rear Power Window Sub Switches. <RH drive vehicles>


M14203000927ENG0000010001


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the front passenger's or rear <Long wheelbase> power window does not work by means of the respective power window sub switch, the power window sub switch or the power window motor may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the front power window sub switch (LH), rear power window sub switch (RH) <Long wheelbase> or rear power window sub switch (LH) <Long wheelbase>
  • Malfunction of the front power window motor (LH), rear power window motor (RH) <Long wheelbase> or rear power window motor (LH) <Long wheelbase>
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the power window main switch.


Check that the power window lock switch is turned off.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Turn off the power window lock switch.

STEP 2. Determine a trouble spot.

Front passenger's door : Go to Step 3.
