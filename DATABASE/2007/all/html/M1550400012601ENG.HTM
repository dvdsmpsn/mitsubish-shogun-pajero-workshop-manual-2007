Code No. B1031,B1032: A/C pressure sensor system


M15504000126ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


This code is set when the A/C pressure sensor circuit is short (Code No.B1031) or is open (Code No.B1032).

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the A/C pressure sensor
  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors
  • Malfunction of the automatic A/C control panel (A/C-ECU)

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-08 A/C-ECU connector and A-27 A/C pressure sensor connector





Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Check the wiring harness between D-08 A/C-ECU connector (terminals 30, 24 and 8) and A-27 A/C pressure sensor connector (terminals 1, 2 and 3).







notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connector D-241, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the sensor signal line and earth line for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the wiring harness.
