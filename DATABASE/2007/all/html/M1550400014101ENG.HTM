Code No.B1055 Motor drive system for rear air mix damper <Dual A/C>


M15504000141ENG0000010000




COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


This code is set when the rear air mixing damper cannot be rotated to the preset opening angle.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the rear air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer
  • Malfunction of the automatic A/C control panel (A/C-ECU)
  • Damaged the wiring harness or connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-08 A/C-ECU connector and E-134-1 rear air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer connector





Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the connector.

STEP 2. Check the wiring harness between D-07 A/C-ECU connector (terminals 51 and 52) and E-134-1 rear air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer connector (terminals 3 and 4).








notePrior to the wiring harness inspection, check intermediate connectors D-219, E-133 and rear heater unit connector E-134, and repair if necessary.

  • Check the motor activating lines for open or short circuit.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 3. Check the rear air mixing damper control motor and potentiometer.
