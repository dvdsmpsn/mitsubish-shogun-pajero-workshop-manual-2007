Inspection Procedure 5: "-" is Displayed on the Ambient Temperature Display.


M15410800393ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The CAN bus line wiring harness, connector(s), or the A/C-ECU may be defective.

POSSIBLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of A/C-ECU
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics

Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the CAN bus lines. (Refer to GROUP 54D - Troubleshooting .) After diagnosing the CAN bus lines, go to Step 3.
STEP 2. M.U.T.-III other system diagnosis code

Check if an A/C system diagnosis code is set.

Q. Is the diagnosis code set?

Refer to GROUP 55 - Troubleshooting , and then go to Step 3.
Replace A/C-ECU and then go to Step 3.
STEP 3. Retest the system

Check if the ambient temperature displays normally.

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

The procedure is complete.
Go to Step 1.
