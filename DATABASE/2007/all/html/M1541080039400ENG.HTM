Inspection Procedure 6: The Radio and CD Player or CD Changer Information cannot be Displayed or the Display is not Normal.


M15410800394ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The radio and CD player or CD changer operation screen will be frozen if the centre display unit or multi display station have received abnormal data from the radio and CD player or CD changer and via M-bus communication.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of radio and CD player or CD changer
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Connector check: D-211 radio and CD player or CD changer connector, D-106 multi centre display connector




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the defective connector.

STEP 2. Check the wiring harness between D-211 radio and CD player or CD changer connector terminal No.9 and D-106 multi centre display connector terminal No.2.




Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the wiring harness.

STEP 3. Retest the system


Confirm that the radio and CD player or CD changer operation screen is displayed normally.

Q. Is the check result satisfactory?

The procedure is complete.
Go to step 1.
