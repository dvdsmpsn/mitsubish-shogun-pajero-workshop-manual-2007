SUCTION CONTROL VALVE CHECK


M11303000904ENG0000010000



1. Disconnect the suction control valve connector.
2. Measure resistance between terminal No. 1 and No. 2
Standard value: Approximately 2.1 Ω (at 20 °C)
3. If not within the standard value, replace the supply pump assembly. After replacing the supply pump, learn the supply pump (Refer to GROUP 00 - Precautions Before Service - What The Common Rail Engine Learns ).
