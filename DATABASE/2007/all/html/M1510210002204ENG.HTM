REMOVAL SERVICE POINT


<<A>> WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY REMOVAL



1. Remove the windshield wiper motor assembly mounting bolts.
2. Use a flat-tipped screwdriver to separate the clamp arm of the windshield wiper motor assembly and the link assembly, and then remove the windshield wiper motor assembly.
cautionThe automatic stop angle is determined at the time that the clamp arm and the windshield wiper motor assembly are installed together, and so they should not be disassembled unless required. Make markings on the vehicle body before removing the windshield wiper motor assembly.
