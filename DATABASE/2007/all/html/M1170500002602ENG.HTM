GENERAL INFORMATION (EXHAUST GAS RECIRCULATION SYSTEM)


M11705000026ENG0000010000


The electronically controlled exhaust gas recirculation system consists of an exhaust gas recirculation valve, engine-ECU and sensors.
The exhaust gas recirculation valve aims at reducing the amount of NOx (nitric oxide) discharge that increases with the rise in the exhaust gas temperature. Thus, the engine-ECU optimally controls the exhaust gas temperature in accordance with the operating conditions of the engine.
To further improve the exhaust gas reduction performance, the system provides the engine-ECU feedback on the exhaust gas recirculation valve opening. Thus, it effects exhaust gas recirculation control to optimize the operating conditions of the engine.
