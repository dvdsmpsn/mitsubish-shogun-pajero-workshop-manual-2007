INSPECTION PROCEDURE 9: After start-up of the engine, the ASC OFF switch cannot be operated. (ASC is fixed in the OFF position or cannot be turned OFF.)


M13506200070ENG0000010000




OPERATION



  • The active stability control system can be turned OFF by pressing the ASC OFF switch for 3 seconds or more with the ignition switch "ON" (after start-up of the engine).
  • When the ASC OFF switch is pressed and held for 15 seconds or more with the ignition switch "ON" (after start-up of the engine), ASTC-ECU judges it as an operation error of the ASC OFF switch and keeps the active stability control system in ON state until the ignition switch is turned ON next time.

COMMENT ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


ASTC-ECU failure or the ASC OFF switch circuit failure is suspected.

PROBABLE CAUSES


The most likely causes for this case are:

  • Damaged wiring harness or connector
  • Malfunction of the ASC OFF switch
  • Malfunction of the ASTC-ECU
