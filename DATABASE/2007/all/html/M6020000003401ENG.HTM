B9. CHANGE GEAR OIL IN TRANSFER CASE (4WD)


M60200000034ENG0000010000



1. Remove the filler plug.
2. Remove the drain plug and drain the oil.
3. Tighten the drain plug to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 32 ± 2 N·m
4. Fill with specified oil till the level comes to the lower portion of oil filler plug hole.

    Specified transfer oil:

      Gear oil API classification GL-4 SAE 75W-85W

    Quantity: 2.8 L
5. Tighten the oil filler plug to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 32 ± 2 N·m
