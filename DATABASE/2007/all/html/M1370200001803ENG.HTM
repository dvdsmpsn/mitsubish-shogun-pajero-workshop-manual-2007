STEERING WHEEL RETURN TO CENTRE CHECK


M13702000018ENG0000010000


Conduct a road test:
1. Make both gradual and sudden turns and see by your own feeling that the steering effort and returnability have no difference between the left and right turns.

2. At a vehicle speed of approximately 35 km/h, turn the steering wheel 90°, hold a few seconds, then release. If the steering wheel then returns 70° or more, the return can be judged satisfactory.
noteThere will be a momentary feeling or heaviness when the wheel is turned quickly, but this is not abnormal. (Oil pump discharge amount is especially apt to be insufficient during idling.)
