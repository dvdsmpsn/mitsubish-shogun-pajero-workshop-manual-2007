NOTE ON REPAIR WORK


REMOVAL



1. When removing the rear floor sidemember extension, the rear sidemember reinforcement and the rear floor sidemember reinforcement B interfere with it, and thus remove the rear sidemember reinforcement and rear floor sidemember reinforcement B in advance.

INSTALLATION



1. When assembling the new quarter inner extension lower parts, apply in advance body sealant to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.

2. When assembling the new rear floor pan parts, apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent

3. When assembling the new rear floor pan third seat parts, apply in advance adhesives to the areas shown in the figure of the instructions.
Adhesive: Epoxyayresin adhesive
Brand: 3M ATD Part No.8115 or equivalent
