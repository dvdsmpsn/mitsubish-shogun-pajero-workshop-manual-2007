VALVE INSPECTION


M11103000906ENG0000010002



1. Check the valve face for correct contact. If incorrect, reface using valve refacer. Valve seat contact should be maintained uniform at the center of valve face.
2. If the margin is smaller than the service limit, replace the valve.
Standard value: 1.0 mm
Limit: 0.5 mm

3. Measure the valves total length. If the measurement is less than the limit, replace the valve.
Standard value:
    Intake: 115.4 mm
    Exhaust: 115.4 mm
Limit:
    Intake: 114.9 mm
    Exhaust: 114.9 mm
