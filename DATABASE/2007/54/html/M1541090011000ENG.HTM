Inspection Procedure F-4: The Headlamp Automatic Levelling Warning Lamp does not Illuminate.


M15410900110ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the power supply circuit, the earth circuit and the communication circuit are normal.


OPERATION


If the height sensor or the headlamp levelling unit is defective, the headlamp automatic levelling warning lamp illuminates.

COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


If the headlamp automatic levelling warning lamp does not illuminate under the illumination conditions, the warning lamp power supply system may be defective, or the combination meter, the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU, or wiring harness and connectors between the two may be defective.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of the headlamp automatic levelling-ECU
  • Defective combination meter
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check the automatic levelling


Check that the automatic levelling operates normally.
