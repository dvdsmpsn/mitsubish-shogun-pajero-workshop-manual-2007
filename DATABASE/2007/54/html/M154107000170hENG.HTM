Inspection Procedure 1: The M.U.T.-III cannot communicate with the immobilizer-ECU. <3,200>


M15410700017ENG0000010000


cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the communication circuit is normal.

COMMENT ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM


The immobilizer-ECU communicates with the M.U.T.-III via CAN communication. Therefore, communication error of the immobilizer-ECU is suspected.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Malfunction of immobilizer-ECU power supply and earth
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics

Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 2.
Repair the CAN bus line (Refer to GROUP 54D - Troubleshooting ).
STEP 2. Check the immobilizer-ECU power supply circuit and earth circuit.

Refer to Inspection Procedure 4 "Check the immobilizer ECU power supply circuit and earth circuit" .

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3.
Repair the power supply and earth circuit to the immobilizer-ECU.
STEP 3. Retest the system.

Check that immobilizer-ECU communicates with the M.U.T.-III.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Intermittent Malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
