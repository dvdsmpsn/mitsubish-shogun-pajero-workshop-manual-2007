B2. CHECK SUSPENSION ARM BALL JOINTS FOR PLAY, AND DUST COVERS FOR DAMAGE


M60200000027ENG0000010000


BALL JOINTS FOR PLAY



1. With the vehicle unladen, check the top end of tyre for the amount of movement.
2. Jack up the lower arm and move the bottom of tyre to check the amount of movement.

DUST COVERS FOR DAMAGE



1. Press the lower arm ball joint cover with your finger to check that there are no cracks or damage in the lower arm ball joint cover.
2. If the lower arm ball joint cover is cracked or damaged, replace the lower arm ball joint assembly.
noteIf the lower arm ball joint cover is cracked or damaged, it is possible that there may also be damage to the ball joint.
3. Press the upper arm ball joint cover with your finger to check that there are no cracks or damage in the upper arm ball joint cover.
4. If the upper arm ball joint cover is cracked or damaged, replace the upper arm ball joint assembly.
noteIf the upper arm ball joint cover is cracked or damaged, it is possible that there may also be damage to the ball joint.
