TRANSMISSION CONTROL

  • A selector lever with two operation gates (a main gate and a manual gate) has been adopted.
  • The A/T indicator panel has a design which is integrated with the floor console to increase the feeling of high quality.
  • The selector lever knob has a pull-button locatd underneath the knob. This pull-button prevents mis-operation and improves selector lever operation and parking brake lever operation when using the armrest.
  • An A/T mis-operation prevention mechanism (shift lock mechanism and key interlock mechanism) has been adopted to prevent the vehicle from starting off suddenly due to a selector lever mis-operation.
