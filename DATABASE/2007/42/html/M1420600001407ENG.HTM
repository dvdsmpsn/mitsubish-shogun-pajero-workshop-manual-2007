REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS


<<A>> SHAFT/DRIVE CABLE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL



Draw out the drive cable assembly as shown in the illustration, then remove the shaft and the drive cable assembly.

<<B>> DEFLECTOR ASSEMBLY REMOVAL



Pry out the deflector assembly using a flat-tip screwdriver as shown in the illustration.

<<C>> REMOVAL OF DEFLECTOR/DEFLECTOR LINK ASSEMBLY
