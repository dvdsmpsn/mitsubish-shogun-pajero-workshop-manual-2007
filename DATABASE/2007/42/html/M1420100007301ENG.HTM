ADJUSTMENT OF HOOD LEVEL AND HOOD STRIKER ENGAGEMENT


M14201000073ENG0000010001


1. Remove the radiator grille (Refer to GROUP 51A, Radiator Grille ).
2. Remove the hood latch cover (Refer to ).

3. Loosen the hood latch mounting bolts but do not remove them. Move the hood latch up/down and left/right to align the hood level and adjust the hood striker engagement.
4. After the adjustment, tighten the hood latch mounting bolts.
5. Install the hood latch cover (Refer to ).
6. Install the radiator grille (Refer to GROUP 51A, Radiator Grille ).
