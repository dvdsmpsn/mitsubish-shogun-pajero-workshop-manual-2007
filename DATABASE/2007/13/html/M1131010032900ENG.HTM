Inspection Procedure 16: Odor, White Smoke, Black smoke and high-concentration CO/HC during idling


M11310100329ENG0000010000


COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM



  • Failure is possibly caused by improper air-fuel ratio, deteriorated catalyst, and failed ignition system or fuel system, or improper compression pressure, and so on.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed air-fuel ratio control system
  • Failed ignition system
  • Failed fuel system
  • Failed intake and exhaust air system
  • Failed exhaust gas cleaning system
  • Improper compression pressure
  • Deteriorated catalyst
  • Failed engine-A/T-ECU
