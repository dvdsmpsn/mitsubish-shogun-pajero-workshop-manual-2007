ALTERNATOR CONTROL


M21302000250ENG0000010000




During engine idle operation, engine-A/T-ECU controls duty of conduction between alternator G terminal and ground. (G terminal duty is controlled to be the same as ON duty of the power transistor inside the voltage regulator). If headlights etc. are turned on while engine is idling, the consumed current increases suddenly, but by gradually increasing the alternator G terminal OFF duty, engine-A/T-ECU restricts sudden increase in alternator's output current and output current is increased only gradually. (Battery current is supplied to the headlamp etc. till alternator generates sufficient current.) Thus, engine-A/T-ECU prevents change in idle speed due to sudden increase of engine load.
