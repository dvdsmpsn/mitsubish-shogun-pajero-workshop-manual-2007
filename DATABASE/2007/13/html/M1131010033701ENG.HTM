Code No. P0171: Right Bank Abnormal Fuel System (Lean)


M11310100337ENG0000010000


OPERATION



  • Refer to Code No. P0201: No. 1 Injector System .
  • Refer to Code No. P0203: No. 3 Injector System .
  • Refer to Code No. P0205: No. 5 Injector System .

FUNCTION



  • If the fuel system goes out of order, the fuel correction value will become larger.
  • The engine-A/T-ECU checks whether the fuel trim value is within a specified range.

TROUBLE JUDGMENT


Check Conditions
  • Engine coolant temperature is lower than 100°C when the engine is started.
  • Intake air temperature is lower than 60°C when the engine is started.
  • Under the closed loop air/fuel ratio control.
  • Engine coolant temperature is higher than 76°C.
  • Air flow sensor output is 8 g/sec or more.
Judgment Criterion
  • Long-term fuel trim has continued to be higher than +12.5 % for 5 seconds.
or

  • Short-term fuel trim has continued to be higher than +7.4 % for 5 seconds.

PROBABLE CAUSES



  • Failed intake air temperature sensor
  • Failed air flow sensor
  • Failed No. 1, No. 3 and No. 5 injector
  • Air drawn in from gaps in gasket, seals, etc.
  • Failed engine coolant temperature sensor
  • Failed barometric pressure sensor
  • Exhaust leak
  • Incorrect fuel pressure
  • Injector circuit or connector damage
  • Failed engine-A/T-ECU

DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE


STEP 1. Check for exhaust leaks

Go to Step 2 .
Repair.

STEP 2. Check for intake system vacuum leaks.


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 3 .
Repair.

STEP 3. M.U.T.-III data list



  • Refer to Data List Reference Table .

    1. Item 05: Intake air temperature sensor
    2. Item 06: Engine coolant temperature sensor
    3. Item 08: Manifold absolute pressure sensor
    4. Item 10: Air flow sensor

Q. Are the check results normal?

Go to Step 4 .
Perform the diagnosis code classified check procedure for the sensor that has shown an abnormal data value (Refer to Inspection Chart for Diagnosis Code ).

STEP 4. M.U.T.-III data list



  • Refer to Data List Reference Table .

    1. Item BB: Barometric pressure sensor

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 5 .
Replace the engine-A/T-ECU.

STEP 5. Connector Check: B-28 injector intermediate connector



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 6 .
Repair or replace the connector.

STEP 6. Perform resistance measurement at B-28 injector intermediate connector.




  • Disconnect connector, and measure at harness side.

    1. Resistance between terminal No. 7 and No. 8 (No. 1 injector).
    2. Resistance between terminal No. 7 and No. 9 (No. 3 injector).
    3. Resistance between terminal No. 7 and No. 10 (No. 5 injector).
OK: 10.5 - 13.5 (at 20°C)

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 9 .
Go to Step 7 .

STEP 7. Connector Check: Injector connector



  1. B-119 (No. 1 injector connector).
  2. B-120 (No. 3 injector connector).
  3. B-121 (No. 5 injector connector).

Q. Are the check results normal?

Go to Step 8 .
Repair or replace the connector.

STEP 8. Check No. 1, No. 3 and No. 5 injector itself.





  • Check No. 1, No. 3 and No. 5 injector itself (Refer to ).

Q. Is the check result normal?

Check and repair harness between injector intermediate connector and injector connector.

  1. Check and repair harness between B-28 (terminal No. 8) injector intermediate connector and B-119 (terminal No. 2) No. 1 injector connector
  2. Check and repair harness between B-28 (terminal No. 9) injector intermediate connector and B-120 (terminal No. 2) No. 3 injector connector
  3. Check and repair harness between B-28 (terminal No. 10) injector intermediate connector and B-121 (terminal No. 2) No. 5 injector connector

  • Check output line for damage.
Replace the injector.

STEP 9. Connector check: D-212 engine-A/T-ECU connector



Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 10 .
Repair or replace the connector.

STEP 10. Check harness between injector intermediate connector and engine-A/T-ECU connector.





  1. Check harness between B-28 (terminal No. 8) injector intermediate connector and D-212 (terminal No. 1) engine-A/T-ECU connector.
  2. Check harness between B-28 (terminal No. 9) injector intermediate connector and D-212 (terminal No. 14) engine-A/T-ECU connector.
  3. Check harness between B-28 (terminal No. 10) injector intermediate connector and D-212 (terminal No. 2) engine-A/T-ECU connector.

  • Check output line for damage.

Q. Are the check results normal?

Go to Step 11 .
Repair the damaged harness wire.

STEP 11. Fuel pressure measurement.



  • Fuel pressure measurement (Refer to Fuel Pressure Test ).

Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 12 .
Repair.

STEP 12. Check for entry of foreign matter (water, kerosene, etc.) into fuel.


Q. Is the check result normal?

Go to Step 13 .
Replace the fuel.

STEP 13. Replace the B-119, B-120 and B-121 injector.




  • After replacing the injector, re-check the trouble symptoms.

Q. Is the check result normal?

Check end.
